50 Cent sues business owner and MedSpa for using photo to allegedly promote business

 Posted on October 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  602 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

50 Cent has accused a Miami business owner of profiting from a photo he took with her and falsely implying that he was a client who underwent a penile enhancement procedure.

The rapper and entrepreneur (born Curtis James Jackson III) has taken his grievance to the courts and filed a lawsuit against Angela Kogan and her businesses, Perfection Plastic Surgery and MedSpa.

The In Da Club star claims that he took the photo with Kogan back in February 2020 because he thought she was a fan.

But since then, he's alleges that Kogan and her companies have used the picture to promote business, according to the lawsuit obtained by TMZ.

Fifty went on to claim in the suit that Kogan, who runs the Perfection Plastic Surgery And Medical Spa in Miami, first used the image to promote the plastic surgery clinics on social media.

'Thank you @50cent for stopping by the number one med spa @bh_perfection_medspa #50cent #bhperfectionmedspa #perfectionmedspa #medspa #celeb #vip #facial #laser #power,' she wrote in the caption of the initial post back on February 3, 2020.
