Luke was born on October 17, 1984, in Santa Cruz, California, to Steve and Diana Rockhold. The most interesting thing is that his father was an athlete and played professional basketball in Europe. What is the fighter’s ethnicity? Luke Rockhold is of German, Irish, and English descent. He gets this heritage from his extended family, as his great grandfather was a full-blood Native American.

Rockhold attended Soquel High School. When he entered the seventh grade, he became part of the school's wrestling team. He continued the sport while in Soquel High School, taking jiu-jitsu classes.

Luke’s career

How did Luke become a big name in UFC? The fighter got interested in wrestling and jiu-jitsu at a young age. Luke tried wrestling for a local community college and chose to take jiu-jitsu full-time.

He won a few BJJ tournaments and later entered mixed martial arts. In 2006, Luke was invited to train at the American Kickboxing Academy in San Jose, California. His impressive talent caught the eye of the head coach Javier Mendez who saw him as a potential future champion.

Rockhold made his professional debut in July 2007, fighting against Mike Martinez for the Melee on the Mountain promotion. He won the fight through armbar submission during the first round. Luke’s second fight was against Tony Rubalcava, and he won through a TKO.

Luke has participated in various competitions throughout the years, accumulating numerous wins that elevated his reputation in mixed martial arts. For instance, shortly after his wins in 2007, he was recruited into the up-and-coming promotion Strikeforce where he made a great impression. Unfortunately, and for unknown reasons, Luke was eliminated from the bout.

The professional fighter returned to the ring after a few years off to win the middleweight title in September 2011. In 2013, after the Strikeforce organization closed, Luke joined UFC and faced Vitor Belfort in his first fight. Unfortunately, he lost the fight in the first round.
