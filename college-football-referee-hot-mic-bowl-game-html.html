Tough days at the office are typically not broadcast on national television, but referee Ed Ardito had one of those during a college football bowl game. 

During the First Responder Bowl taking place in Dallas, Texas between Rice and Texas State, Ardito had a tough time with two particular announcements.

First, Ardito was announcing a timeout in the third quarter that he initially said was called by Texas State and was a 30 second timeout.

But, in reality, it was a media timeout. Once, Ardito realized that, he was caught on a hot mic screaming 'Damnit!' and then correcting himself.

Later in the game's fourth quarter, Ardito spent some significant time correcting a penalty he announced.

Turns out, there were three penalties on the play - with two of those offsetting - and Ardito spent some time explaining the situation.

Fans on Twitter offered some sympathy to the referee, with one saying that he looked like he needed 'a scotch and some paid time off'.

The First Responder Bowl had issues outside of the refereeing gaffe - with some on Twitter complaining that the Texas State side of Gerald Ford Stadium was out of alcohol before the end of the first half.

By the time the third quarter had come and gone, all alcohol in the stadium was apparently sold out.

Texas State beat rice 45-21.
