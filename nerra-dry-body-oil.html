One of the best ways to keep your skin hydrated during the heat of summer is to invest in a dry body oil; it sinks in fast and doesn’t leave behind a sticky residue like thick creams often do. Plus, many body oils often carry transformative ingredients that go above and beyond simply hydrating dry areas—like this Mediterranean-inspired dry body oil from NERRĀ.

The nourishing formula is made up of a blend of potent botanical oils, such as argan oil, hibiscus seed oil—which is known as the “Botox plant” due to its high level of antioxidants— and macadamia seed oil, that together firm sagging areas while protecting the skin against outside aggressors. Unlike other body oils, this fast-absorbing one comes in a mess-free, spray bottle for easy applications. The brand recommends spritzing it first into the hands or directly onto the skin after a hot shower while the skin is still warm.
