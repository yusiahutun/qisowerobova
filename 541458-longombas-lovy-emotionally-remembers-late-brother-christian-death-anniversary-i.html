Lovy Longomba Emotionally Remembers Late Brother Christian on His Death Anniversary: &quot;I Miss You&quot;

 Posted on April 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  608 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • US-based singer-turned-preacher Prophet Lovy Longomba disclosed that the day his brother Christian Longomba was tough for him
  • Christian passed away soon after a valiant battle with a brain tumour that was diagnosed when the pair relocated to the US
  • Lovy and Christian made up the beloved singing duo The Longombas, known for hits like Vuta Pumzi and My Queen

Popular preacher Prophet Lovy Longomba, who was one half of the singing duo The Longombas, emotionally paid tribute to his late brother Christian Longomba on the anniversary of his death.

What happened to Christian Longomba?

Christian, the second member of the Longombas, passed away on March 13, 2021.

He had been diagnosed with a brain tumour shortly after he and his brother moved to the United States.

His death was confirmed by his elder brother, Lovy, in a sombre Facebook post.

"To be absent from the body is to be present with the Lord. I love you, Christian - my brother, my friend, and my partner - but Jesus loves you more. Enjoy Heaven," he wrote.

Prophet Lovy pays tribute to Christian Longomba

Three years down the line, Lovy is still struggling to recover from the loss of his beloved brother.

The singer-turned-preacher took to Instagram to remember Christian in a heartfelt post.

He disclosed that Christian's death had dealt him a heavy blow and that he missed him greatly.

He wrote:

"03/13/2021 was one of the most difficult days of my life but the Grace of God kept me. I miss you Christian."

Netizens condole with Prophet Lovy

The post resonated deeply, sparking a wave of support from followers and celebrities alike, who offered their condolences and joined Lovy in honouring Christian's' memory.
