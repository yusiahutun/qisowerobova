An Iconic Two-Stroke Machine

The Aprilia RS 250 is widely regarded as one of the most iconic two-stroke motorcycles ever produced. Its powerful engine, sleek design, and thrilling performance have made it a favorite among motorcycle enthusiasts worldwide.

Championship-Winning Pedigree

The RS 250 has an impressive racing history, with numerous championships under its belt. It has dominated the 250cc class in Grand Prix motorcycle racing, showcasing its exceptional performance and handling on the race track.

Engineered for Speed

The RS 250 is equipped with a high-revving 249cc two-stroke engine, capable of delivering impressive power and acceleration. Its lightweight construction and advanced technology make it a true speed demon on the road.

Perfect Balance

The Aprilia RS 250 boasts a perfectly balanced chassis, allowing for precise handling and agility. Its superb suspension setup and responsive brakes ensure optimal control, even during high-speed maneuvers.

Lightweight and Nimble

With a dry weight of just over 140 kilograms, the RS 250 is incredibly lightweight. This, combined with its compact design, makes it incredibly nimble and agile, providing an exhilarating riding experience.

Aerodynamic Design

The sleek and aerodynamic design of the RS 250 not only enhances its visual appeal but also improves its performance on the road. The wind tunnel-tested fairings and streamlined bodywork reduce drag, allowing for higher top speeds.

Carbon Fiber Components

To further enhance its performance, the RS 250 features carbon fiber components, including the front mudguard and exhaust silencers. These lightweight materials contribute to improved handling and overall weight reduction.

Exclusive Limited Production

The Aprilia RS 250 was produced in limited numbers, adding to its exclusivity and desirability among motorcycle collectors. Its rarity makes it a sought-after model for avid enthusiasts.

Built for the Track

The RS 250 is a true track-focused motorcycle, designed to excel on the racing circuit. It features high-performance components, including adjustable suspension, racing brakes, and a close-ratio gearbox.

Innovative Power Valve System

The RS 250 incorporates an innovative power valve system, which optimizes engine performance at different RPM ranges. This technology ensures a smooth power delivery and maximum torque throughout the rev range.

Classic V-Twin Sound

The Aprilia RS 250’s two-stroke V-Twin engine produces a distinct and exhilarating exhaust note that adds to its appeal. The combination of the engine’s power and the unique sound it produces creates a truly thrilling riding experience.

Legendary Riders’ Choice

Throughout its history, the RS 250 has been a popular choice among professional riders. Renowned motorcycle racers, including Valentino Rossi and Max Biaggi, have praised its performance and chosen it as their preferred racing machine.

Global Recognition

The Aprilia RS 250 has gained global recognition and has become an iconic motorcycle across continents. Its reputation for exceptional performance and thrilling performance has made it a favorite among motorcycle enthusiasts worldwide.

Collector’s Item

Due to its limited production and racing heritage, the RS 250 is highly sought after by motorcycle collectors. Owning one is like owning a piece of motorcycle racing history that represents the pinnacle of two-stroke engineering.

Enduring Legacy

The Aprilia RS 250 has left an enduring legacy in the world of motorcycles. Its combination of power, performance, and distinctive design has solidified its place as a legendary two-stroke machine that continues to captivate motorcycle enthusiasts to this day.

FAQs

1. What is the Aprilia RS 250?

The Aprilia RS 250 is a 250cc two-stroke sport motorcycle produced by the Italian manufacturer Aprilia. It was first introduced in 1994 and gained popularity for its racing pedigree and exceptional performance.

2. What makes the Aprilia RS 250 unique?

The Aprilia RS 250 stands out for its lightweight construction, aggressive styling, and powerful two-stroke engine. This combination of factors creates an unrivaled riding experience for enthusiasts who crave speed and precision.

3. What is the top speed of the Aprilia RS 250?

The Aprilia RS 250 has a top speed of around 140 miles per hour (225 kilometers per hour). Its lightweight design and high-performance engine allow for impressive acceleration and speed on both the track and the road.

4. Is the Aprilia RS 250 street legal?

Yes, the Aprilia RS 250 is street legal in most countries. However, it’s important to check the specific regulations and requirements of your region to ensure compliance with local laws regarding bike modifications and emissions.

5. Can I still buy a new Aprilia RS 250?

No, the Aprilia RS 250 is no longer in production, and new models are not available. However, used models can still be found on the market through private sellers or specialized motorcycle dealers.

6. How much does the Aprilia RS 250 cost?

The price of a used Aprilia RS 250 can vary depending on factors such as its condition, mileage, and year of manufacture. On average, you can expect to pay between $7,000 and $12,000 for a well-maintained RS 250.

Remember that statistics and prices mentioned in this article are subject to change over time, so it’s always advisable to do thorough research or consult with experts before making any purchasing decisions.
