See 'Flip or Flop' Star Christina Hall Stuns in a Sequin Mermaid Dress

 Posted on March 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  354 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Christina Haack Hall has left us all stunned after walking the pink carpet this weekend at the Barbie World Premiere in Los Angeles.

The glamorous event fell on the Christina on the Coast star's 40th birthday, so of course she had to show up and show out, and we have to say her appearance was definitely not a flop! Christina walked the carpet with her new husband Josh Hall before viewing the new blockbuster. She wore a show-stopping form-fitting, pastel evening dress with side cutouts and an ultra-high slit.

Rodin Eckenroth//Getty Images

Christina, in her sequin dress and silver cage high heels, matched perfectly with husband Josh, who was in a navy suit that had a Barbie-pink pocket square. Christina's pink nails and blonde curls really put the look over the top, transforming her into a real-life Barbie (mermaid or Malibu, we can't decide).
