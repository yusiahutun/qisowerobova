It looks like Blake Lively is trying to shed her good girl image.

The blonde sported giant butterfly tattoos on her back and arms as she filmed scenes for her upcoming movie Savages in Los Angeles today.

Indeed, with her beaded arms and flowers in her hair, she looked more like a hippy than her It girl character Serena van der Woodsen in hit show Gossip Girl.

Luckily for fans of the starlet, the tattoos were just temporary transfers that were applied for her performance.

The sexy star also got to cool down in the blazing summer heat as she drove a stylish red Mini convertible with the top down.
