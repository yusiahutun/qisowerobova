Who is Zanjoe Marudo?

He is a Philippine actor and model known for Maalaala Mo Kaya. He was born in Tanauan, Batangas, Philippines on July 23, 1982. Who is the father of Zanjoe Marudo? Zanjoe's father is Zosimo Marudo from Batangas, and his late mother, Rosanna Acuesta, is from Calauag.

The celebrity was raised in Batangas alongside his four siblings, Zosanne, Zandro, Zjaye, and Zaniejean. After completing high school, he attended San Sebastian College to perfect his acting career.

From elementary school until high school, he played on the basketball varsity team. His sports side was influenced by his brother Zandaro. Zanjoe was in the San Sebastian College Staglets, playing in the NCAA.

Career

Zanjoe's career consists of acting and modelling, and he has numerous awards to his credit. He started his career as a model after high school and appeared in the Bench Fever fashion show. The celebrity also entered a modelling contest, Body Shots, and won the first runner-up title.
