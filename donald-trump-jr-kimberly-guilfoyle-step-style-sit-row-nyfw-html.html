Donald Trump Jr and Kimberly Guilfoyle step out in style as they sit front row for NYFW

 Posted on February 18, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  804 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Donald Trump Jr and girlfriend Kimberly Guilfoyle made their way from the White House to the runway on Wednesday for New York Fashion Week. 

The loved up duo sat front row at the Zang Toi show at Springs Studio in New York City on Wednesday evening. 

The Malaysian designer's show was America-themed, featuring outfits in red, white and blue and skirts draped with the nation's flag. 

The couple were joined in the front row by Marla Maples, Donald Trump's second wife, and Fox News anchor Bill Hemmer.
