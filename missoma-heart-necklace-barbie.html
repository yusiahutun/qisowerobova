How to Shop the Missoma Heart Necklace from 'Barbie'

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  431 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

If you're among the millions of movie-goers who've seen the Barbie movie, you may already recognize this gold heart-shaped necklace. Like Barbie tickets themselves, the exact Missoma necklace from Barbie the Movie sold out in the blink of an eye. But there's good news: Barbie's heart necklace is back in stock—but certainly not for long.

Missoma Ridge Heart Charm Necklace

Missoma Ridge Heart Charm Necklace

Credit: Missoma

Strung from a dainty bobble chain, the 18K gold vermeil pendant features Missoma's signature ridge detailing and a twisted rope jump ring. Toward the end of the film, the titular Barbie, played by Margot Robbie, wears the gold heart necklace in Barbieland with a sweet yellow dress. So consider it the perfect piece to top off your Barbie-inspired outfits or gift to a die-hard Barbie fan.

Barbie viewers were quick to spot that Barbie's heart necklace isn't the only Missoma jewelry that the film's costume designer, Jacqueline Durran (who also fashioned Spencer, Beauty and the Beast, and Greta Gerwig's Little Women) featured in the film. America Ferrera wore the brand's Double Chain Necklace; Alexandra Shipp, who plays 'Celebrated Author Barbie,' wore this Jelly Heart Gemstone Charm Necklace in promotional shots.

Off-screen, Margot Robbie also wore Missoma jewelry long before she was cast as Barbie. She's been a fan of the climate-conscious British jewelry brand for years. The gold Engravable Roman Arc Coin Necklace she wore to a 'Women in Film' pre-Oscars party and the gold Large Horn Necklace she wore while surfing in Southern California are both still available. While you're at it, browse the brand's other celeb-approved jewelry styles, including Missoma earrings, bracelets, and necklaces regularly spotted on Kate Middleton, Jennifer Lopez, Hailey Bieber, and more.

If you're serious about your love of all things Barbie, you'll need to act fast before the Missoma jewelry from Barbie sells out again. Since the film's release on July 21, anything and everything Barbie-worn has been flying off shelves, so you haven't got a second to waste.
