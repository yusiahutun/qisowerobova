15 wall unit designs and ideas to spruce up your living room

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1462 words  |  Larita Shotwell

When transforming your living room into a stylish and functional space, wall units play a pivotal role in aesthetics and organisation. These versatile pieces of furniture provide ample storage and serve as focal points that can elevate the overall look of your living area. From sleek modern designs to rustic charm, wall unit designs have many possibilities to spruce up your living room.

Wall units are furniture units typically mounted on walls and used for storage, display, or entertainment. They come in various styles, sizes, and configurations to suit different needs and preferences.

Some popular and latest wall unit designs in Kenya include floating shelves, built-in bookcases, modular shelving systems, entertainment centres, and display cabinets. Consider the available space, functionality, aesthetics, and material quality when choosing a wall unit design.

Beautiful wall unit designs and ideas

Choosing the right wall unit designs in Kenya for your home can be overwhelming. Nevertheless, here are unique wall unit designs and ideas to inspire your living room transformation:

1. Wooden finish

One of the best wall unit designs for a living room is one with a wooden finish. It is a piece of furniture with shelves, cabinets, and compartments designed to hold a TV and related media equipment. It is made of wood with a polished finish, giving it a sophisticated and elegant look.

A TV wall unit with a wooden finish can add warmth and texture to the room, creating a focal point that enhances the overall decor. Wood's natural aesthetics can also bring a sense of cosiness and elegance to the space.

2. Minimalist floating shelf

A minimalist floating shelf is sleek and modern. It typically has a wall-mounted shelf or cabinet holding a television. It is simple in design and without unnecessary ornamentation or decoration.

This type of wall unit design idea can create a clean and uncluttered look in a room. The TV is mounted on the wall rather than on a bulky stand, making the space more open and spacious. Also, the design can be a practical solution for small spaces, as it takes up minimal floor space.

3. Modular storage cubes

Modular storage cubes are versatile furniture pieces that allow customisable storage and display options. They consist of individual cube-shaped compartments arranged in various configurations to fit different spaces and storage needs.

Modular storage cubes add visual interest and depth to a wall unit design. Mixing and matching different cube sizes and shapes allows you to create a unique and personalised look that complements your overall decor style.

4. TV wall mount

A TV wall mount is among Kenya's simple wall unit designs today. It is a bracket or frame that attaches to the wall and secures a TV in place. Incorporating a TV wall mount into a wall unit design idea can help save space and create a modern, streamlined look.

It also prevents the TV from being knocked over or damaged. The design allows for easy adjustment and positioning for optimal viewing angles.

5. Gallery wall

A gallery wall is a modern design concept where a TV and a curated collection of artwork, photographs, and other decorative items are mounted on a wall. It serves both functional and aesthetic purposes. For example, it creates a focal point showcasing the TV and its artwork.

6. Plant wall

A plant wall refers to a wall unit design idea that incorporates a TV unit and a living plant wall. It combines the functionality of a TV wall unit with the aesthetic appeal and health benefits of indoor plants.

A plant wall adds a natural element to the overall design, creating a more inviting and relaxing atmosphere. It can also help soften the look of a large TV screen, blending technology seamlessly with nature.

7. Industrial pipe shelving

Industrial pipe shelving is a unique TV unit design you should try today. It combines the strength and durability of industrial piping with the functionality of a TV wall unit.

The design idea uses exposed pipes, often in a black or raw metal finish, which support shelves or other storage components. The result is a sturdy and stylish piece that can serve as a TV stand, media unit, bookcase, or console cabinet.

8. Built-in bookshelves

A built-in bookshelf combines a TV stand with shelving units for storing books, decor, and other items. This wall unit design idea maximises space efficiency, provides a cohesive and organised look, and serves as a multifunctional piece of furniture.

In other words, you can showcase your TV while displaying your favourite books, decor items, or family photos in one integrated unit.

9. Ladder shelf

Using a ladder shelf, you can decorate a long wall in a living room. The unit combines a ladder shelf with a space designated for a TV. The ladder shelf typically has multiple shelves placed at different heights, resembling a ladder to display decorative items or store books and other belongings.

The idea adds a sense of verticality to the space, making the room feel spacious and visually interesting.

10. Wall-mounted desk

A wall-mounted desk combines a desk, a TV stand, and storage space into one unit mounted on the wall. The design is ideal for small spaces, as it saves floor space and provides a functional and stylish solution for displaying a TV and storing related equipment.

The wall-mounted desk can be a workspace, while the TV wall unit can store and display media components and books.

11. Geometric shelving

Geometric shelving is among the various types of wall units in modern homes. It is a type of wall unit design idea that incorporates geometric shapes, such as squares, rectangles, and hexagons, into the design of the shelving and storage units.

The design creates a modern and stylish look for a TV wall unit and provides functional storage space for media equipment, books, and decorative items.

12. Accent lighting

Accent lighting is a design concept that uses lighting to highlight and enhance the aesthetic appeal of a TV wall unit. Various lighting techniques can achieve this, such as wall washing, downlighting, or ambient lighting.

Accent lighting is used in a TV wall unit design to create a visually appealing and inviting atmosphere, minimise eye strain, and elevate the viewing experience.

13. Rustic wood shelves

Rustic wood shelves incorporate wooden shelves into the wall-mounted unit where the TV is placed. These shelves are typically made of natural wood, adding a warm, rustic touch to the living space. The shelves' natural wood tones and textured surfaces bring a sense of comfort and charm to a room, making it feel inviting and homey.

14. Hidden storage

A hidden storage TV wall unit is a type of wall unit design that integrates a TV into a storage system, allowing the TV to be concealed when not in use.

The design is popular because it maintains a clean and uncluttered living space while providing a functional storage solution for your TV. One way to achieve a hidden storage TV wall unit is to conceal the TV with sliding panels or cabinet doors.

15. Mirrored backing

A mirrored backing is a wall unit design idea that involves using mirrored glass as the backing material, with the TV being the central focus of the unit.

The design concept uses mirrors to create a sense of depth and visual interest while providing a functional solution for hiding wires and other electronic components.

Final word

Wall unit designs offer a versatile and stylish solution for organising your living room. With various materials, finishes, and configurations available, you can choose a wall unit that fits your aesthetic preferences and functional needs. Consider incorporating lighting fixtures, open shelving, artwork, and mirrors to further enhance your living room.
