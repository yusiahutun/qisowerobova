Ryan Destiny’s Bio

American actress and singer Ryan Destiny was born on the 8th of January 1995, as Ryan Destiny Irons. Talking about her parents, Ryan is the daughter of Deron Irons and Dawn Irons.

Her father Deron is a member of the 90s R&B group Guests and is of African American descent whereas her mother is of mixed ethnicity (White and African-American).

Ryan developed an interest in music at a very young age.

Following her dream, in the year 2011, Ryan auditioned for America's Got Talent with her musical trio New Limit. She formed the trio with Jasmine Pore and another friend. However, she gained popularity only after she began to perform as the member of the girl-group Love Dollhouse in 2013.

Too Busy For A Boyfriend?

On the American drama series, Star, Destiny is romantically linked with her co-star Quincy Brown.

But, who does she spend her time with when not in front of the camera? The man in question is none other than American actor and model, Keith Powers. They met for the first time at the Teen Vogue party in 2015. However, they have only been dating for three years as of 2019.

Ryan and Keith have been the "it" couple for the black community showing that black love can and does exist.

Career In Acting

Talking about Destiny’s acting career, she began appearing on the screen since 2010 with The Wannabes Starring Savvy. Later in 2013, Destiny appeared in Low Winter Sun with a recurring character of April Geddes.

Destiny then portrayed the character of Grace in a 2015 independent film A Girl Like Grace.

Destiny also had the opportunity to be a cast on the hit musical drama Empire. However, she had to turn the role down because of her contract with All Def Music. She is currently seen in Star, where she portrays the character of Alexandra Crane.
