A popular Irish bar in Florida has been banned from flying the Irish flag just days before St Patrick's Day.

The owners of Culhane's Irish Pub in Atlantic Beach, northeast Florida, have been told they are no longer allowed to fly the flag because of a local rule banning the commercial display of non-U.S. flags despite displaying it for eight years.

The four sisters who own the bar said they would apply for a temporary permit to at least fly the green, white and orange flag on St. Patrick's Day but are shocked by the ruling.

Ban: Local officials have warned this Irish flag must be taken down outside the Culhane's pub in northeast Florida

Flagged: City officials in Atlantic Beach wrote this order to the owners of Culhane's Irish bar. It orders them to take down the flag just days before St Patrick's Day

'St. Patrick's Day is huge for us,' said Aine Culhane, who owns the pub with sisters Mary Jane, Michelle and Lynda.

They were handed a citation issued by code enforcement officers for the City of Atlantic Beach on February 20, giving them 24 hours to 'cease display of flags other than American flag.'

The Culhanes said they had flown an Irish flag and an American flag on the front of their pub for eight years and were unaware of the ordinance.

'I couldn't believe it,' Aine Culhane said.

'I'm shocked,' her sister, Michelle, told First Coast News. 

'This is America, the land of every nationality. I couldn't believe they made us take down our Irish flag.'

Shocked: Michelle Culhane, pictured, one of pub's owners, said she was surprised by the flag ban

Popular bar: Culhone's, pictured, will not be able to fly the Irish flag on March 17

'We never break the law. We were just shocked and kind of sad that we couldn't hang the flag...Everybody is upset about it.'

City officials did not immediately return phone and email messages seeking comment.

Acting Mayor Maria Mark told television station WTLV that the city council was reconsidering the ordinance but she doubted any changes could be approved before St. Patrick's Day on March 17
