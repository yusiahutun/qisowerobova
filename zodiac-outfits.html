12 Zodiac Sign Outfits That Are Astrologist-Approved

 Posted on May 17, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  697 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

When cultivating a personal sense of style, your horoscope might influence your fashion choices more than you realize. Are you a risk-taker who likes to turn heads? An introvert who prefers the classics? Perhaps you reside comfortably in the middle, occasionally pushing the envelope but never fully going over the edge. Although seemingly arbitrary, it's possible that your sartorial preferences actually fall closely in line with your sun sign — so why not embrace it?

To help get you started, we turned to astrologer Lisa Stardust, who laid out what wardrobe pieces, fashion trends, and overall vibe each astrological sign tends to gravitate toward. From Sag's statement-making wardrobe to Taurus's fashionable yet functional OOTDs, we've rounded up our favorite looks for each sign below.

Keep scrolling to discover our favorite zodiac outfits that will help develop your unique sun sign style.

Aries

Athleisure might as well be the Ram's middle name. 

"A chic, short-sleeved top and skirt sweatsuit is ideal for Aries' fiery sentiments," Stardust told us. "It'll be perfect for their sporty vibe." 

So go ahead and bring back the early aughts with a Paris Hilton-inspired tracksuit this season — besides, it's all back in style now anyway.

Taurus

It's time for Bulls to embrace the inner head-turner that's buried (perhaps not-so-deep) in all of them. 

"Luxurious Taurus will enjoy wearing faux leather dresses or skirts that tug at their curves," said Stardust.

These pieces will be easy to dress up or down, especially with sneakers and T-shirts.

Gemini

"The twin star will like versatile looks that can double for day or evening wear," Stardust suggested. "A morning-to-night trend worth indulging in? "They won't shy away from sparkle 24/7."

Cancer

"Layers are perfect for Cancer," Stardust revealed, adding that those born under this sign "like to protect their hearts and energy to ensure happiness." 

If you want to take things up a notch, consider mixing prints while you're at it.

Leo

For this sign, a single statement piece is all that's needed. 

"Bright and cropped pants will allow Leo to stand out in a crowd," said Stardust. 

What's more? This spotlight-stealing piece of clothing will allow them to easily make a dramatic entrance.

Virgo

Love your knitwear? 

"A simple sweater dress with boots is a great no-frills and sophisticated look for Virgo," Stardust told us. 

We recommend a pair of wear-anywhere Dr. Martens for the cooler months and cushy slides come spring and summer.

Libra

"Monochrome sets and outfits will help Libra maintain and find their balance this spring," said Stardust. 

Plus, thanks to the likes of our favorite celebs, the monochromatic trend has continued to remain strong.

Scorpio

Scorpio style can be summed up in three words: timeless and edgy. 

"A classic black slip dress and combat boots are not only sexy but totally on point for Scorpio's goth tendencies in fashion," noted Stardust.

Sagittarius

The Archer may decide to stick with comfort without skimping on style. Our recommendation? Give your basics a boost, similar to your favorite off-duty model. 

"A cool rock and roll tee with leggings or bike shorts will help Sag embrace their free and edgy spirit," suggested Stardust.

Capricorn

Capricorns, listen up: "Find comfort in wearing a more conservative and chic look, like a polo dress in a cool print," recommended Stardust. Consider it the personalized Margot Tenenbaum look that you've always been searching for.

Aquarius

Ready to go all out? Now is most definitely the time. 

"Aquarius should try a bold-printed dress that expresses their individuality and personality," said Stardust. 

You can't go wrong with something leopard (it's pretty much a neutral), but if you're feeling whimsical, opt for stripes, polka dots, or even a '90s daisy design.

Pisces

We've officially reached the 12th and final zodiac sign, which is inherently soulful. 

"The mystical sign Pisces should opt for flowy and floral dresses that are pretty and comfortable," advised Stardust. It keeps with their dreamy and bohemian spirit.
