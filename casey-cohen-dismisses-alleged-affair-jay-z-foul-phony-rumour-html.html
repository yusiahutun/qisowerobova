She was rumoured to have had a affair with Jay Z.

But reality star Casey Cohen, who starred in the Bravo series Princesses: Long Island, has denied reports that she has slept with the singer, while she is launching a legal battle.

Casey, 28, was named in several US magazines as a woman who had gotten close to Jay Z.

Denial: Long Island star Casey Cohen has rubbished rumours linking her to Jay Z

The reality star's lawyer is reported to have contacted the magazine's publisher, Bauer Media, with a cease and dismiss letter, rubbishing the claim as 'a foul and phony mistress-rumor,' TMZ reported.

Casey reportedly admitted in the letter that she has met Jay Z and his wife Beyonce, but only through her job as a VIP hostess in New York.

She is believed to have demanded an apology from Bauer, and for all posts about her to be removed.   

Close-knit family: Beyonce and Jay Z land in New York on Sunday with their two-year-old daughter Blue Ivy

  

It was reported earlier this month by a Bauer publication that hip-hop star Jay Z had allegedly been spending time with New York hostess Casey Cohen, who he reportedly saw at least twice a month.

The allegations came less than a month after a video surfaced of Beyonce's sister attacking Jay Z in an elevator at the 2014 Met Gala – supposedly for having a girlfriend on the side.

The cheating rumours are new for the couple, who are extolled as having one of the best marriages in music.

The couple are currently preparing for their joint On The Run tour, which begins in Miami on June 25, with sell-out tours throughout the States.
