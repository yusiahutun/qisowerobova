Britney Spears Looks Happy and Carefree in New Nude Selfies

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Britney Spears is baring it all after a major win in her ongoing conservatorship case.

The pop star shared a series of completely nude self-portraits on Instagram yesterday, following the news that her father, Jamie Spears, had officially been suspended as the conservator of her estate. The new photos were taken during Spears's trip to what is believed to be Hawaii with her fiancé, Sam Asghari, and gave a glimpse of the tropical paradise the couple are enjoying during their stay.

"Playing in the Pacific never hurt anybody 😉💋🙊 !!!! Pssss no photo edits … the tub curves 😂😂😬😬 !!!" Spears wrote alongside the set of photos.

Some of Spears's close friends, including heiress and reality star Paris Hilton, cheered on the pop star in the comments section.

"Love seeing you so happy and free! 🔥 You deserve it! 💯 Love you B😍," Hilton wrote in an encouraging note.

Earlier in the week, Spears shared video clips of her and Asghari taking a ride in a prop plane, with Spears posting that she was on "cloud 9" after her latest court hearing.

According to E! News, Spears is "very happy" about the news that her father can no longer legally control her personal or professional life thanks to the recent court filing. Spears's next court date is set for November, when Judge Brenda Penny will reportedly decide whether or not the conservatorship should stay in place.

"This is a substantial step toward her freedom," Spears's lawyer Mathew Rosengart said during a press conference. "It's been a lot of hard work. It's been intense. I'm proud. Britney's proud."
