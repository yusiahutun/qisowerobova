Chris Henchy's biography

Christopher Thomas Henchy was born on 23rd March 1964 in New York City, United States. He is 57 years old as of 2021. He holds the American nationality and belongs to the Caucasian white ethnicity.

The producer's parents are Michael Henchy (father) and Patricia Henchy (mother). He joined Saint Mary on the Hill Catholic School for his elementary studies. After completion, he joined Friendswood High School, where he graduated in 1982.

He was actively involved in journalism while in high school. He was also a member of the yearbook staff. After completing his elementary and high school studies, Chris enrolled at the University of New Mexico.

The star took a Bachelor of Science Degree in Finance. While at the university, he was a member of the Sigma Alpha Epsilon chapter.

Career

Chris began his career after graduating from the university. He relocated back to his birthplace, New York City and began working on Wall Street. After working for a short while, he opted to quit his job and start working in comedy.
