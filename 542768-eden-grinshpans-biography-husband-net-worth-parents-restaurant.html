Who is Eden Grinshpan?

She is a Canadian TV presenter, chef, restaurateur, and blogger born in Toronto, Canada, on August 18, 1986. She is the daughter of an Israeli father and a Canadian Jewish mother.

Eden Grinshpan's parents are Riva and Menashe Grinshpan. Sisters Arielle and Renny Grinshpan are her two siblings who live in Aviv. Ever since Eden was a child, her family has supported her cooking passion.

Growing up, Eden's dad always encouraged her to try new foods. She later learned that exploring the foods of the countries she visited was a great way to learn about the people and their culture. Sunday brunch with her family was always her favourite, with warm fresh bagels, smoked salmon, and cream cheese.

In 2004, the celebrity graduated from Metropolitan Preparatory Academy. She later attended Culinary School at Le Cordon Bleu in London, where she studied Pastry and Cuisine, graduating with a Grand Diplome.

Career

According to Cooking Channel, after completing her studies, Eden travelled to India, where she volunteered with different organizations and taught locals about cooking and healthy eating.

She began her career at a gym's front desk before landing a job at a creperie in Toronto, where she spun crepes. Her first experience in a professional kitchen came when Eden moved to London, England, to work in the pastry department of Providore. She then worked at an Italian restaurant.

Grinshpan's love for travel and food took her backpacking through India and Israel, where she gained invaluable culinary experience, including a stint in the kitchen at Nana. After relocating to New York City, she worked at the front of the house at Babycakes.

In her travels and cooking, the chef and television host has been committed to exposing the world to the food she relates to most: contemporary Middle Eastern and modern Israeli cuisine.

At 27, Eden was recognized on the esteemed Forbes 30 under 30 list. Her career in television kicked off when she sold her show to the Cooking Channels, which included:

  • Eden Eats
  • Log on

She has also been a judge on reality TV shows like:

  • Doughnut Showdown
  • Sugar Showdown

According to the New York Post, Eden co-founded a restaurant, DEZ, in Nolita, NYC, with Esquared Hospitality. Although it has since been closed, it remains a testament to her entrepreneurial spirit.

In 2020, Eden released her debut cookbook, Eating Out Loud, bringing Middle Eastern food into everyday living through a stylish and approachable lens. She is set to release her second cookbook, Tahini Baby, in March 2025, promising it to be a love letter to her favourite flavour.

Currently, she hosts the 11th season of Top Chef Canada. Eden's relatable personality has made her a favourite among luxury and lifestyle brands, such as Crate and Barrel, Gap, Sezane OXO, and others, who engage her for endorsements, recipe development, and appearances.

Ido Niv-Ron and Eden Grinshpan's marriage is blessed with two daughters, Ayv and Romi.
