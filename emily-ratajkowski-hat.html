It’s always a struggle to stay warm while looking cute, but not if you’re Emily Ratajkowski. The starlet recently stepped out in the streets of New York wearing a burnt orange fluffy hat. While some hats can ruin an outfit, EmRata’s fuzzy hat proves you don’t have to sacrifice warmth for style. Her fluffy hat looked both chic and cozy—perfect for anyone questioning what to wear in thewinter.

Ratajkowski paired her fuzzy hat with an oversized leather jacket, a black fitted skirt, and chunky platform boots. The outfit felt chic and elevated, without compromising the much-needed coziness we all look for in the colder months. The hat offered a dose of ’90s flare that feels exactly on-trend. And the chunky boots balanced out the fluffy hat, keeping it from dominating the look.

EmRata’s street style always captures the it-girl, model-off-duty aesthetic—simple and effortlessly cool. And this fuzzy hat outfit was no exception. Ratajkowski has worn the hat and others like it on many occasions. She’s paired the burnt orange hat with a black leather jacket, a pink fluffy hat with a red windbreaker and cargo pants, and a black fuzzy hat with a zebra print coat and matching mini skirt.

While EmRata is known to wear high-end name brands, you can often score her looks for a fraction of the price. And after seeing her style that fuzzy hat like a pro, I became dedicated to finding dupes of the versatile winter must-have. 

While real fur hats can cost upwards of $300—and are very controversial—you don’t have to spend that much to get Emrata’s fuzzy hat look. After tireless searching, I’ve found some affordable fluffy hat dupes to keep you feeling warm and looking chic for less than $35. And if you want the real thing, you can score her Emma Brewin bucket hat for just over $500.
