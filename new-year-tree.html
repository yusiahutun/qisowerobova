New Year Tree Decorating Ideas

 Posted on February 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  250 words  |  Martina Birk

Balloon Tree

This tree is all about the countdown! You'll find celebratory accessories like glasses and party hats inscribed with the upcoming year, and balloons stamped with times (it's a part of a fun game) on this glitzy statement tree.

Get the tutorial at Overstuffed.

Tiara Tree

Strip your tree down to the needles and lights, and then adorn it with dollar store tiaras and noise makers. It doesn't get easier than that!

Get the tutorial at Domestic Fashionista.

Party Hat Tree

Teeny timepieces, a party hat tree topper, and shimmering gold and silver ribbon will let your guests know where the party's at.

Get the tutorial on Home Is Where the Boat Is.

Tinsel Tree

This tinsel tree is the perfect way to dress up your living room for a fancy fete. Here blogger Jennifer Perkins outfitted her tree with champagne flutes, party hats, noise makers, and more festive trinkets.

Get the tutorial on Treetopia.

Antique Clock Tree

Heather Gatherer/The Perfect Gathering

This blogger removed some of the larger bulbs from her tree and replaced those empty areas with antique and wooden clocks.

Get the tutorial at The Perfect Gathering.
