It is not the kind of dress you expect to see on the Oscars red carpet.

But then, Brandi Glanville is not the kind of star you expect to see there either.

The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills star left fashion commentators searching for the appropriate response in a plunging gown which she reportedly designed herself.

What a dress! Brandi Glanville designed her plunging gown herself, all the better to show off her surgically enhanced breasts as she arrived for the Oscars in Hollywood on Sunday

The dress very nearly revealed more than Brandi intended - or perhaps that was the plan.

The mother-of-two certainly seemed delighted with the effect as she posed one hand on hip, grinning at the cameras.

Are you looking LeAnn? Brandi shows off her figure

But it was the chest area that drew the eye, revealing as it did a fair amount of her surgically enhanced breasts, for which she paid $11,500.

It's not the only part of her anatomy that Brandi has undergone surgery to perfect, with the reality star also shelling out to have her vagina 'rejuvenated'.

The 40-year-old former model makes a living from her reality stardom, and is currently a New York Times bestselling author with her book Drinking And Tweeting.

High risk: But as a former fashion model Brandi knew exactly what she was doing

But it is as the ex-wife of Eddie Cibrian for which she is most famous.

Eddie left her for LeAnn Rimes after they were publicly caught cheating on their respective spouses in early 2009.

LeAnn and Brandi are arch enemies, and their mutual dislike has lead to many disagreements, which  they have shared with fans on their Twitter pages.

Getting in on the act: While Brandi was posing on the red carpet, her ex-husband spent the night with LeAnn Rimes, who tweeted 'My hot date tonight.... Love him!'

While Brandi was at the Oscars Eddie and LeAnn were also dressing up for the occasion.

LeAnn tweeted a picture of herself and Eddie tweeting: 'My hot date tonight.... Love him!'

She spent the evening as a guest on Piers Morgan's CNN OScars show, later writing: 'Going back home to quickly get into pj's and out of these heels.
