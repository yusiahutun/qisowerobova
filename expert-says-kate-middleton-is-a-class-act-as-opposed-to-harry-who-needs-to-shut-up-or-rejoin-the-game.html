Expert says Kate Middleton is a 'class act' while Harry needs to 'shut up' or rejoin the 'game'

 Posted on February 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  361 words  |  Martina Birk

Kate Middleton may not have been born with a princess title, but a royal expert thinks she is shining with grace and authenticity as the years pass.

The Princess of Wales is often referred to as the epitome of class and royalty by experts. Earlier this year, True Royalty TV co-founder Nick Bullen commended the steeliness of Kate Middleton and praised her strong character. The expert talked about how the mother of three has transitioned from being Prince William‘s young bride to a ‘key player’ in the royal family.

Journalist praises Kate Middleton’s class advising Harry to choose a side

2023 has been a significant year in the Harry and Meghan versus the Firm drama as followers started to lose hope that the duo will find their way back to the palace. The year started with Harry’s explosive memoir Spare, which aired the ‘dirty laundry‘ of the royals.

After months of strategic silence, there were rumors about Harry and Meghan joining the Christmas celebration of the Firm. But sources say that the invitation might have been withdrawn after Omid Scobie’s Endgame was released.

Now, as the distance between Harry and the palace keeps increasing, journalist and author Robert Jobson shares that Kate stands as a class act as opposed to Harry, who was born a prince.

“I think the King is winning and she is winning, and I think Meghan and Harry need to either rejoin the game or shut up,” Jobson said.

She serves the crown with such style and authenticity

Royals are known for having to keep low profiles as part of the protocols and Jobson shares that Kate’s authenticity and style shine through everything she does, especially how she serves the crown.

Despite many shocking claims in Endgame, Jobson says that Kate does not think about them: “She is perfectly natural. She has spent so much time with the mum and the kids, she is a genuine person you know.”

Speaking of her personality, Jobson says that the credit not only goes to the royal family but the Middleton family as well, as she has become one of the “main stars of the royal family.”
