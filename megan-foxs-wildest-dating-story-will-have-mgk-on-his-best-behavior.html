Megan Fox's 'wildest dating story' will have MGK on his best behavior

 Posted on April 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  534 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

After the release of her book, Pretty Boys Are Poisonous, Megan Fox opened up about some of her previous relationships, including one of the worst things she did to an ex. She also revealed how she was ‘addicted’ to falling in love with her co-stars.

The star is now engaged to rapper, Machine Gun Kelly. The two have been together ever since meeting on the set of their movie, Midnight in The Switchgrass in 2020. Although there were some ‘rumored blips’ in their Hollywood relationship, the pair now seem stronger than ever.

Megan Fox reveals one of the ‘worst things’ she did to an ex

The Jeniffer’s Body star recently appeared on the Drew Barrymore show. Megan Fox was discussing her previous relationship, she was asked about the worst thing she’s done to one of her exes.

Unfortunately for fans, she wasn’t willing to spill the juicy details, as she responded: “I don’t know if that’s something I can say on TV.”

However, she did provide a story from her wild dating days, and it sounds just as dramatic.

“When I was young, I did have a temper and I was wild, I got mad and I took a bunch of paint and I painted a Friedrich Nietzsche quote all over [this guy’s] wall, so he had to repaint his house afterward,” she recalled.

Apparently, it was a furious quote. “It was like a really angry quote about, like, how life is futile, you suck basically. So he had to repaint the bedroom,” the 37-year-old concluded.

The star was ‘addicted’ to falling in love

Carrying on from her early dating days, Megan Fox also admitted that she was addicted to falling in love. She said she had a habit of falling for “every new costar” in the early days.

Megan confessed: “I was addicted to falling in love, and I think I probably hurt a lot of people in that process too because a lot of people have been in love with me and I did not respect or honor that.”

However, she revealed everything changed when she became a mother to her three children.

The actress has three sons, Noah Shannon, 11, Bodhi Ransom, 9, and Journey River, 7, whom she shares with her ex-husband, Brian Austin Green.

The celebrity couple had an ‘on-off relationship’ from 2004-2009, before getting married in 2010. They confirmed their split in 2020, with the divorce being finalized in Feb 2022.

Earlier this year, she had her tattoo dedicated to Brian covered up.

A look back at Megan Fox’s ex-boyfriends

Megan is now happily engaged to Colson Baker, aka. MGK, but before that, she dated a couple of Hollywood stars, starting with 7th Heaven actor, David Gallagher.

The two dated from 2003-2004, when her career first kicked off. However, it was fairly short-lived.

She was then rumored to be dating Transformers co-star, Shia LaBeouf, in the mid 2000s, although they kept it on the down low.

In a 2018 interview on Watch What Happens Live, she confirmed the past ‘showmance’ by saying: “Um, I mean, I would confirm it was romantic. I love him, I’ve never been really private about that. I love him.”
