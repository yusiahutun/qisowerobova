Who is Bill Ayers:

Bill Ayers is a famous Politician. He was born on December 26, 1944 and his birthplace is Illinois. Bill is also well known as, Leader of the Weather Underground political protest movement who was famously called a terrorist by conservatives in 2008. He was a longtime professor at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

Bill is originated from United States. During the 2008 US presidential campaign, a controversy arose over his contacts with candidate Barack Obama, with much of the right-wing claiming that he was a terrorist.
