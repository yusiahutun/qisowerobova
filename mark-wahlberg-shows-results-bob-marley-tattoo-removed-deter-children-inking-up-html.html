Former bad boy Mark Wahlberg has certainly turned over a new leaf.The singer turned actor recently revealed that he wanted to get his tattoos removed to deter his own children from getting inked.

And yesterday on the beach in sunny Florida he revealed the results of the painful sessions as the Bob Marley tattoo complete with song title One Love has all but disappeared.

Wahlberg showed off his bulked up frame as he enjoyed a day off the set of his new movie Pain And Gain, in which he plays a body builder.

Mark Wahlberg and the Bob Marley Tattoo in his shoulder, still showing even after the painful removal procedure

Wahlberg, 40, – who has four children Ella, eight, Michael, six, Brendan, three, and Grace, two, with wife Rhea Durham.

He recently revealed that he has taken his two eldest children along to watch him suffer as he is in the process of erasing his tattoos

He said: ‘[All the tattoos] have meaning to me, but it’s both personal and professional. I don’t want my kids getting tattoos.

'And I wanted them to be [removed] by the time I did The Fighter because putting make-up on and covering them up has always been a pain in the butt.’

One love: Wahlberg emerges from the sea in Florida yesterday, and seen with the tattoo intact in 2000

He continued: ‘I’ve taken my two older kids to the procedure so they see how painful it is and what I have to go through.'

The star’s etchings are a reminder of his troubled past. The former drug addict claims to have been in trouble with police around 20 to 25 times.

Incidents include an alleged racially motivated attack, a charge of  attempted murder and he has served two years in a Correction House jail.

Family man: Wahlberg enjoyed some downtime with his wife and children during the filming of movie Pain And Gain

The former rapper’s first tattoo was done unprofessionally as a teenager and he claims the image of a shamrock on his ankle was crooked so had Tweety Bird placed over it after moving to Los Angeles.

The rest include a design of his initials ‘MW’ with his surname on his upper right arm, a Bob Marley tattoo with song title One Love on his upper left arm and one which the Roman Catholic holds as his most meaningful.

Mark has a rosary tattooed around his neck, with a crucifix and the words ‘In God I Trust’ resting over his heart.

The father to Ella Ray, eight, Michael, five, Brendan Joseph, three, and Grace Margaret, two, spoke about his markings last month on The Late Show With David Letterman.

He confessed that initially he was told it would take eight to ten laser treatments however he required over 30 and is still in the process of getting them removed.
