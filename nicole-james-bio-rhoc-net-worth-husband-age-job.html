Nicole James On RHOC

On the premiere of season 16 of “Real House Of Orange County”, Nicole James was introduced to the show. After meeting up with her friend Heather Dubrow who has been friends with her for nearly eight years, they realized that they were friends with RHOC star Shannon Beador.

Heather later met with Shannon where she mentioned that Nicole knew her. In the confessional Shannon spoke about a secret she knew about Nicole. But, she didn’t reveal it to Heather instead shared it with fellow RHOC stars Gina Kirschenheiter and Emily Simpson.

The secret was Nicole had sued Heather’s husband Terry Dubrow.

Gina insisted on telling Heather about the lawsuit against her husband. However, Shannon had made a pact with Gina and Emily to keep their mouth shut until thoroughly confirmed if it was the same person. But Gina wasn’t going to follow the pact and confronted Heather about the lawsuit.

Heather couldn’t believe her ears and asked if she was kidding about the lawsuit.

As it turns out, Nicole sued Terry for the mishaps caused during the breast implants. She was allegedly unhappy with the surgery he had performed. This resulted in a feud between two friends.

Heather was the one who brought Nicole for the shoot of RHOC. RadarOnline shared that Nicole stopped shooting RHOC several weeks before the season’s production wrapped in October. It is suspected that because of this reason, Nicole’s appearance on the show was demoted.

Fans of the show on the other hand have their theory. One fan wrote, “I wonder if Nicole is the Danielle Gregorio of this new season of #RHOC. We’ve heard so much about her yet she isn’t even a main cast member or friend. I wonder how much we see of her.”

Similarly, another fan added, “Nicole seems to be in so many photos and scenes yet she’s not even credited as a friend? I need to know what happened.”

Who Is Nicole James Husband?

Nicole James was previously married to her ex-husband Marcel Weise. Together they shared one son named Blake Weise.

Marcel hailed from Huntington Beach, California. He has been living in Newport Beach, California since 2019. He is a Marina High graduate who completed his college education at the University of New Mexico in 1988.

Marcel worked for West Coast Capital Group, Inc.

Blake is a college student who turned 23 years old in October 2021. Back in 2019, Nicole shared that her son was studying in Germany. He graduated from Corona Del Mar High in 2017.

Nicole also has a daughter named Presley James from a different relationship. Presley was born to her on 24 September 2009. She turned 12 years old in September 2021. Nicole is a doting mother for both of her kids. She appears to be raising Presley as a single mother.

Proud parent, Nicole shares about her daughter’s life on her socials. It appears, Presley is interested in horseback riding and even competed and has won in several events. However, she never opened up about the father of her daughter.

But, on a different page, RHOC star Shannon revealed that Nicole was in a relationship with Kid Rock. They began seeing each other after his split from Pamela Anderson. But, Nicole wasn’t on the show for long to confirm this piece of information.
