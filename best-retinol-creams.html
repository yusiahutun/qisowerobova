Best Retinol Cream for Sensitive Skin

Glossier Universal Pro Retinol

Best Retinol Cream for Sensitive Skin

Glossier Universal Pro Retinol

Pros
  • Vegan
  • Safe for sensitive skin
Cons
  • May not be intensive enough for some skin types

Containing 0.5 percent retinyl sunflowerate—retinol derived from sunflower seed fatty acids—plus stevia leaf extract, this cream is one of the gentlest on our list (and even deemed safe for sensitive skin). While it's effective at smoothing lines and wrinkles, it won't leave you feeling dry, since its formula is packed with humectants, like glycerin, to hydrate your complexion. That said, you may need another layer of moisturizer on top, especially if you have dry skin.

Customer Review:

"I was in search of a retinol product and I’m glad I found this one. I followed the instructions to apply at night a few times a week and gradually move up. I’m now using almost every night. I have very sensitive skin and this has not bothered me at all." —Chloe S.
