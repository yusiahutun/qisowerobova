Astrological predictions and key dates for 2023 according to a psychic healer

"Whilst social media has been bombarding us with how to make vision boards, set intentions and Manifest great things for the New Year, you’re most definitely not alone if you’ve found the beginning of 2023 incredibly slow and a tad like pushing water uphill," explains fourth generation psychic Estelle Bingham.

Estelle has been described as 'The Heart Whisperer', and she has been supporting others to find more love, purpose and connection in their lives for over 20 years. Praise for her practice comes from high-places and she regularly works with socialites, fashion editors and influencers, "There’s only one word for Estelle Bingham’s practice: transformative. Or maybe two: utterly transformative," explains Gwenyth Platrow’s Goop. So who better than Estelle to turn to for ask for astrological predictions and key insights for the year ahead?

What can we expect in 2023?

"Astrologically Mars, the planet of action, courage and motivation and Mercury the planet of communication have both been retrograde since the end of last year and so this time is actually about resting, nourishing and very gently preparing for what’s to come.  Because 2023 is going to be quite extraordinary, full of endless adventure and possibility so buckle up and enjoy the ride."

The Super New Moon in 2023

Esoterically and energetically the Super New Moon of January 21st feels like the moment when this year officially kicks off, so you really have until then to finish journaling those dreams and intentions. Not only would Mars and Mercury have gone direct by then, so no more wading through treacle, but it also heralds the beginning of the Chinese New Year which is all about positivity, success and good luck.

Relationships in 2023

"The Year of the Water Rabbit brings us hope with a capital H. Rabbit medicine invites us to build strong relationships in love and work and helps us achieve great things with gentle determination.  If balance and peace have previously eluded you, this is most definitely your year." 

Transformation in 2023

"The cherry on the cake for me is when Pluto exits Capricorn and moves into the expansive Air sign, Aquarius in March. The last time Pluto was in Aquarius was between 1778 and 1798, and ushered in a period of huge social change all over the world.  Pluto is all about deep transformation and eliminating what’s no longer working in our lives, whilst progressive Aquarius, is really able to help us envision our future and build for it. Aquarian energy is deeply rooted in our shared humanity, community and connection.  It is powerfully idealistic and rebellious, encouraging us to ‘think outside of the box’ and robustly challenges our imagined fears and limitations."

6 ways to harness the power and magic of 2023 according to Estelle Bingham:

1. You still have time to start that vision board. Don’t over-complicate it… you can just as easily write a list of manifestations in your journal.

2. Creativity is a big word this year, either for birthing new ideas, or where you might have lost hope around a project or dream, now is the time to trust in your unique gifts once more. 

3. If there are relationships that no longer serve you, and are not quite your ‘vibrational match’, 2023 will force your hand…it’s time to let them go.  

4. Stay open to adventure, remain curious and seek experiences that broaden your horizons.

5. Recognise the value of your fulfilling friendships and social networks.

6. 2023 buzzwords are Collaboration, Integration, Adventure and Abundance.
