Jairo Martinez (Bassist) - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on February 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  177 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Mexican bassist player famous for his work with the band Yahritza y Su Esencia. His band's debut single, "Soy El Unico," was released in 2022 and reached number 1 on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs chart and number 20 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. His musical fame has led him to amass more than 700,000 followers on Instagram, where he posts career updates and lifestyle content. 

Before Fame

He began posting videos of himself playing the bajoloche on TikTok when Yahritza y Su Esencia was just starting out. 

Trivia

"Soy El Unico" has racked up tens of millions of streams on Spotify since its release. The official music video for the track has gained in excess of 60 million views on YouTube. The TikTok videos that his band released attracted the attention of Ramon Ruiz, CEO of Lumbre Music, which led to a record deal. 

Family Life

His parents are agricultural laborers from Mexico. His sister, Yahritza Martinez, is the lead vocalist for Yahritza y Su Esencia. 

Associated With

He and his bandmates covered "Esta Dañada" by Ivan Cornejo.
