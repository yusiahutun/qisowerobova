    How to Use Best CeraVe Products for Acne

    Clogged pores are frequently the main factor in acne and breakouts. The buildup of dirt, bacteria, dead skin cells, and excess oil causes pores to clog, resulting in little bumps or blemishes on the skin, known as comedones (4).

    By using the correct skincare routine and making the right lifestyle choices, you can manage and control acne. For instance, refrain from pinching or squeezing spots to prevent possible scarring. Leverage skincare products with active ingredients that are gentle but helpful in treating acne and hydrating the skin.

    The acne-fighting skin care products will be different for everyone based on the skin condition, skin type, and problem areas, but here are the 3 basic steps to include in your CeraVe acne treatment routine:

    Step 1: Cleanse

    The first step is cleaning the surface you are working on. Whether it's the entire body or just the face, start by using an effective cleanser. For example, normal skin types can opt for CeraVe Foaming Facial Cleanser or CeraVe Renewing SA Cleanser. Oily skin types can choose from CeraVe Acne Foaming Cream Cleanser or CeraVe Foaming Cleanser Bar. For dry skin CeraVe Hydrating Cream-to-Foam Cleanser is a good choice.

    Step 2: Hydrate

    After cleansing and exfoliating the surface, you would want to hydrate the skin. Serums provide instant hydration to the skin, but usually, they are sticky or feel heavy on the face. The CeraVe Resurfacing Retinol Serum and CeraVe Skin Renewing Vitamin C Serum are fast-absorbing, lightweight, and non-greasy serums that target acne scars and rebuild the protective skin barrier while evening out the skin tone.

    Step 3: Moisturize

    All skin types, including oily and acne-prone skin, need to use a moisturizer daily. A moisturizer seals hydration and rejuvenates the skin. Your daytime moisturizer and nighttime cream must be oil-free and non-comedogenic. CeraVe AM Facial Moisturizing Lotion with Sunscreen is great to wear during the day as it not only keeps the skin hydrated but also adds a layer of SPF that shields the skin from the harmful UVA and UVB rays. For the nighttime, CeraVe Skin Renewing Night Cream is ideal as it helps in reducing fine lines and wrinkles while supporting skin elasticity.

    It is important to understand that though acne is a common skin issue, every person's acne is unique. Getting rid of blemishes and stopping new ones from emerging starts with the awareness of the various forms of acne, including blackheads, whiteheads, papules, pustules, nodules, and cysts, and the best ways to treat each. CeraVe products are formulated on MultiVesicular Emulsion Technology (MVE) that helps in protecting skin barrier, which in turn ensures long-lasting hydration (5). Further, the secret to controlling outbreaks and regaining a clear, healthy-looking complexion is figuring out which type of acne you're suffering and the appropriate skincare products and components to manage it. Making a commitment to a skincare routine that contains tried-and-true, dermatologist recommended components is the key to properly addressing acne troubles.
