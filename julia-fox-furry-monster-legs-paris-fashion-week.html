Julia Fox Wore Giant Furry Monster Legs to Paris Fashion Week

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Julia Fox is no stranger to strange looks—especially during Fashion Week.

The model and muse capped off her whirlwind street style tour in Paris last weekend, when she embraced her wild side in a pair of magnificent furry boots. The outrageous footwear, which was completely covered in shaggy black fur, extended all the way up to her thighs, giving the appearance of monster legs à la Where the Wild Things Are.

Of course, she didn't stop there. The rest of her ensemble was just as eccentric, including zip-up leather underwear and an ultra-cropped leather bomber jacket. For accessories, Fox went with a pair of black rectangular sunglasses and a massive heart-shaped bag covered in matching black fur and adorned with a black collar.

Over the past month, Fox has been reinventing the wheel when it comes to Fashion Week street style. Some honorable mentions include a see-through mesh dress with a belly button piercing cutout and a miniskirt-and-bandeau set made of stacked belts.

Also, Fox's monster legs aren't the first time she added an animalistic dimension to her outfit. While at the Wiederhoeft show in New York City last month, she stepped out in a sparkling hot pink evening gown embroidered with a life-size cartoon poodle. And during another appearance at NYFW, the Uncut Gems actress channeled her inner horse girl in a pair of leather assless chaps with an attached horse tail on the back (her dog-shaped bag featured a matching, swishing tail).
