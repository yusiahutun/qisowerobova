Early life

Talia Jackson is 22 years old. She was born on August 28, 2001, in Madison, City Wisconsin, USA. Her birth zodiac sign is Virgo. Moreover, she grew up along with her younger brother Armani Jackson. Her brother is also a television actor, who is famous for the “Braden Morris” role in Grey’s Anatomy (2014) drama series.

She holds a very good family background. Her dad is a professional Basketball player and star UW basketballer while her mom is a former Model, Actress, writer, and film Producer. Therefore, her first job was singing during a couple of Christmas seasons with Andrea Bocelli and David Foster.
