Dad, His 2 Children Earn Master's Degrees from Same University: We Started and Finished Strong

 Posted on February 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  397 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • A father and his two children received their postgraduate degrees from a branch of Mississippi State University in the United States of America
  • Commondre Cole, his son Ja’Coby Cole, and daughter Iesha Gully graduated with their masters in Education
  • Iesha Gully mentioned in an interview that they are all proud because it's a big accomplishment for the family

A father and his two children have graduated together from a branch of Mississippi State University in the United States of America.

Father and his kids serve couple goals

The trio earned their master's in Education from the university this past Thursday.

Per a report by The Associated Press, Commondre Cole, his son Ja’Coby Cole and daughter Iesha Gully were among 153 who graduated at MSU-Meridian's spring commencement, said Edgainesentertainment.

Commondre Cole is an educator at Northwest Middle School while his son Ja’Coby Cole teaches at Oakland Heights Elementary, both in the Meridian Public School District.

Gully is an educator at Northeast Elementary in the Lauderdale County School District.

Speaking about the family's achievement, Gully said:

"This is a big accomplishment for our family."

Gully told WTOK-TV that they started strong and finished strong.

Mother And daughter graduated from same university

In a similar story, a mother and her daughter from Louisiana who share the same birthday walked on the same stage during their graduation from Grambling State University.

Suzzon Jiles and her daughter, who share more than one thing in common graduated from the establishment in the United States of America on Thursday, May 12.

The 48-year-old mother of two and her daughter Trinity Humphrey shared they were proud of the milestone as reported by MyArklamiss.
