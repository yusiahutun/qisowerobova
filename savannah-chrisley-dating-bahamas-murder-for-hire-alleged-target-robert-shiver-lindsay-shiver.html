Savannah Chrisley is off the market, and her new man has quite the interesting past.

While appearing on The Viall Files Thursday, the Chrisley Knows Best alum revealed she's dating Robert Shiver, a former football star whose estranged wife, Lindsay, has been accused of trying to murder him.

"This guy that I'm talking to, his wife just tried to kill him," Savannah told host Nick Viall, joking, "It's fine."

Lindsay, a former beauty pageant queen, has been in the news for her alleged murder-for-hire plot against Robert.

Despite Robert's complicated past relationship, Savannah insists he's a total "normie."
