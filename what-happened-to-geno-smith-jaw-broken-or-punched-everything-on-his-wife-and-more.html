What Happened To Geno Smith Jaw? Broken Or Punched

The Seahawks quarterback Geno Smith’s jaw was broken after he got punched in the face by his former teammate.

However, Geno couldn’t attend the camp as his close family member had passed away and he later refused to pay back the money to Enemkpali.

This created a heated exchange between the footballers and Smith’s jaw was left broken after Ik punched him in the face.
