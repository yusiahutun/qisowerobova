Who Is Daniella Kertesz? Her Bio and Wiki

Daniella was born on 11th March 1989, in Ramat Hasharon, Israel to her father Gabriel Kertesz and her mother Dorothy. Her father moved from Romania to Israel at the age of nine and her mother moved from Belgium. Kertesz is of Israeli nationality and belongs to white ethnicity.

Kertesz grew up with her brother who is a pilot and her father was also in an anti-aircraft unit in the Israeli Defense Forces. She spent most of her childhood in Yemin Moshe but moved to Tel Aviv in her early teens.

Daniella has not spoken about her early education except that she joined Rubin Academy of Music and Dance to learn dance. She is 30-years-old as of 2019 and stands at a height of 5 feet 4 inches.
