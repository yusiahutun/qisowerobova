Reed Jobs' biography

Reed Paul Jobs was born on 22nd September 1991 in California, USA. He is 30 years old as of 2022. Reed has an American nationality and a mixed racial background. His ethnicity is a combination of Syrian, Swiss, German and Caucasian White.

His parents are the late Steven Paul Jobs (father) and Laurene Powell (mother). His father was an American business magnate, industrial designer, investor, and media proprietor. Some of the positions he held were:

  • The chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), and co-founder of Apple Inc.
  • The chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar
  • A member of The Walt Disney Company's board of director
  • The founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT

His father passed away on 5th October 2011 at his home in Palo Alto, California. He succumbed to complications arising from a relapse of his previously treated islet-cell pancreatic neuroendocrine tumour. The relapse led to respiratory arrest.

On the other hand, her mother is an equally successful personality. She is an American billionaire, businesswoman, and the founder of Emerson Collective. The organization deals with investing and philanthropic activities and advocating for education reform, social redistribution, and environmental conservation.

In addition, she was one of the major donors to Democratic Party politicians, including Kamala Harris and Joe Biden. She is also co-founder and president of College Track, which prepares disadvantaged high school students for their college education.

Reed Jobs has three sisters, two biological and one half-sibling. His biological siblings are Erin Sienna Jobs (born on 19th August 1995) and Eve Jobs (born on 9th 1998). His half-sister is Lisa Brennan Jobs (born on 17th May 1978).
