How long have Brenda Song and Macaulay Culkin been together – In the realm of childhood fame, Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song have transitioned from being well-known child stars to embracing the joys of parenthood. This journey began in 2017 when the “Home Alone” actor and “The Suite Life of Zack & Cody” actress crossed paths on the set of “Changeland” in Thailand. Fast forward to the present, and the couple is now proud parents of two children, with their first child, Dakota Song Culkin, born on April 5, 2021.

A Nod to the Past

Dakota, the couple’s firstborn, carries a poignant namesake. Born on April 5, 2021, in Los Angeles, Dakota Culkin is named after Macaulay’s late sister, Dakota Culkin, who tragically passed away in 2008 at the age of 29. This heartfelt connection adds depth and significance to the name chosen for their first child.

Engagement Following Dakota’s Arrival

Just nine months after welcoming Dakota, the couple took their commitment to the next level. Brenda was spotted with an engagement ring on her left hand in Beverly Hills, California, confirming their engagement. As they ventured into wedding planning, Brenda shared insights into the challenges of balancing this with the demands of being new parents.

Arrival of the Second Child

In December 2022, speculation surrounded the arrival of Macaulay and Brenda’s second child. Macaulay’s brother, Kieran, confirmed the news at the premiere for the final season of “Succession.” The couple welcomed another child, whose name is yet to be disclosed, adding a new chapter to their evolving family story.

Brenda and Macaulay as “Hands-On” Parents

In a January 2022 interview, Brenda shed light on their parenting approach. Despite busy schedules, the couple opted to be “hands-on” parents, forgoing a nanny and relying on Brenda’s mom for support since Dakota’s birth. Brenda emphasized the importance of help during the challenging postpartum period, highlighting the often-overlooked “fourth trimester.”

Connecting Across Coasts

Living on opposite coasts – Macaulay in Los Angeles and Kieran in New York City – posed challenges for family gatherings. Kieran expressed the difficulty of arranging get-togethers, especially with his own young children. Despite the distance, the family strives to maintain connections and bridge the gap between their lives on opposite sides of the country.

Brenda’s Postpartum Struggles

Brenda openly shared her postpartum challenges, acknowledging the pressure to return to work soon after Dakota’s birth. Balancing the demands of a blossoming career with the responsibilities of motherhood proved to be a formidable task. Brenda spoke candidly about the sacrifices she made, including dealing with “FOMO” (Fear of Missing Out) and the emotional toll of being away from her son during significant milestones.

A Family Affair

In December 2023, Macaulay Culkin received his star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, a momentous occasion for the actor and his growing family. Brenda and their two children were present to show their support. This marked a significant chapter in Macaulay’s career and family life, with the actor expressing gratitude for Brenda’s unwavering support and acknowledging her role in their family.
