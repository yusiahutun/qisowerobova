The TNT Champion and an AEW executive, Cody Rhodes, is currently working without AEW contract as a free agent. According to Fightful's Sean Ross Sapp, Cody Rhodes is a free agent, working without an AEW contract and he's also the current AEW TNT Champion.

Rhodes contract had expired at the end of 2021 and hasn't renewed the contract since then, according to the reports. Rhodes is not the only member in his family to be associated with AEW as his wife, Brandi Rhodes is also an AEW executive and on-air talent and his brother, Dustin Rhodes is also working in the company. Moreover, Cody Rhodes is entangled in AEW as he co-owns the Nightmare Factory wrestling training with partner QT Marshall that serves as the defacto training facility for AEW. 

Nevertheless, Cody Rhodes is scheduled to be at Dynamite on Wednesday as he was absent for several weeks with the restrictions of COVID and maybe Cody will sign a new deal with the company in the near future too.

Cody Rhodes' Net Worth And Salary

Cody Rhodes has an estimated net worth of $2 million according to Celebrity Net Worth and his main source of income is through his career as a professional wrestler and actor. Cody Rhodes is also famous for being the son of WWE Hall of Famer Dusty Rhodes and as half-brother of WWE wrestler Goldust.

Cody Rhodes is a 36 year old professional wrestler and also one of the founding members of All Elite Wrestling (AEW). He is also considered one of the hottest superstars in the independent wrestling scene after leaving WWE in 2016. Cody was also the first wrestler to be signed to an AEW contract but recently his contract had expired at the end of 2021. However, he works as both on-screen talent and as the Executive Vice president of Tony Khan's promotion. Cody is also considered one of the highest paid talent in AEW according to various sources and his estimated salary is roughly around $3 million per year.

However, Rhodes had chosen Chris Jericho as the highest paid wrestler in 2019 and said, " I would say that initially with Chris Jericho and the amount of money that he has been paid by AEW, and having the largets contract on the roster - but, in the very beiginning, there were three people that said, 'I want to be the quarterback.' Kenny Omega was one, I was one, and Chris Jericho was one. That sounds liek it's a recipe for disaster perhaps in other worlds, but in the wrestling world, it's a recipe for success."

Cody Rhodes has worked as Cody Runnels from 2006 to 2007 and as Stardust in 2014. He has also used Fuego II as his ring name since 2021 along with Cody Rhodes.  His nick name is The Prince of Pro-Wrestling and The American Nightmare. Cody Rhodes is a 2 times WWE Intercontinential Championship and 3 times WWE Tag Team Championship and also 3 times World Tag Team Championship. Cody is also the 3 times AEW TNT Championship and  IGWP United States Championship, GFW NEX*GEN Championship, NWA  World Heavyweight Championship, WCPW Internet Championship and more.

Cody Rhodes Married Life With Wife Brandi Alexis Reed

Cody Rhodes is married to Brandi Alexis Reed who is also a professional wrestler and ring announcer and a businesswoman. They got married in September 2013. She currently holds the position of Chief Brand Officer in AEW. She graduated from the University of Michigan and was a figure staker for 17 years. She was also a news reporter in Michigan for two years and did some modelling for renowned magazines such as Maxim and Hawaiian Tropic. She got her Master's degree in broadcast journalism from the University of Miami. She has also dome sone acting gigs in the series WAGS Atlanta.

Cody met his wife, Brandi in WWE at first and they started dating when he asked her out for a month. Cody had shared about their starting times and said, "I looked at her ringside and I went from working the 10,000 people to just her....I thought she was the most amazing and beautiful woman."

His wife, Brandi, also said that they started dating randomly and said, "He started asking me to come hang out with him and I said no many times because it's a work environment and I was new at the time and was not looking to rock the boat." She added that she definitely wanted to go out with him and was very into him.

The couple welcomed their first child together, a beautiful daughter named Liberty in 2021. Cody announced the birth of his first child via Instagram and wrote, "6lbs 12oz - Liberty Iris Runnels - Mother & Daughter are doing wonderful." Brandi told, "I want my daughter to be as passionate about something as I am with wrestling. It took me a good 27 years to find that."

His wife, Brandi joined WWE in 2011 and initially performed in Florida Championship Wrestling and made her debut as an NXT announcer.
