    Mila Kunis was keeping her baby bump discreetly under wraps at the premiere of her new film – but there was no mistaking her pregnancy glow.

    The expectant star looked stunning at the Hollywood premiere of . Her blossoming tummy could barely be seen under her flowing black gown, which did – however – show off her toned legs.

    Completing the look was a pair of vertiginous nude-hued platform stilettoes and minimal jewellery. In the absence of her partner Ashton Kutcher, Mila attended with the film's director Paul Haggis and her co-stars Moran Atias, Emanuela Postacchini, and Maria Bello. The movie also stars Adrien Brody, Olivia Wilde, Liam Neeson, James Franco, Kim Basinger, and Maria Bello. It tells the tale of three couples' intertwining love stories and unfolds in in Rome, Paris, and New York. Mila only recently confirmed she is expecting a baby with her former co-star Ashton.
