Kelly Osbourne, Sid Wilson Grammys Photos: Whos Her Masked Date?

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  426 words  |  Aldo Pusey

It might’ve been a little scary to the uninitiated, but Kelly Osbourne & Sid Wilson’s Grammys look made total sense. She made her red carpet debut with her partner, Sid Wilson, after welcoming their first child together in 2022.

Born October 27, 1984, Osbourne is a British-American television personality, actress, singer, and fashion designer. She is the daughter of rock legend Ozzy Osbourne and his wife Sharon Osbourne. She gained widespread recognition through her appearance on the reality television show The Osbournes, which aired from 2002 to 2005 and focused on the daily lives of the Osbourne family. Apart from her television career, Osbourne has dabbled in music, releasing her debut album Shut Up in 2002. She has also worked as a fashion designer and was known for her distinctive style.

Kelly Osbourne and Sid Wilson’s Grammys Look

Like we said, Kelly Osbourne and Sid Wilson’s Grammys look was definitely shocking but given that Sid is a member of the iconic metal band Slipknot, it was all par for the course. Slipknot are known for their rather terrifying masks, even becoming known as the Masked Nine. The reason for this is that they decided, early on, that they wanted the focus to be entirely on their music and performance rather than their famous faces.

Speaking with AP on the Grammys red carpet, Kelly revealed it was the couple’s first date night since their baby and their first time walking the red carpet together. “I could not be more proud than I am,” she said. “I’m so proud of her,” Sid continued. “You’re always learning and you’re always teaching. It never stops, so to know that we can continue to get better and learn new things and to always evolve, no matter what. That’s the best.”

The couple celebrated their son’s first birthday in November 2023 and Kelly honored Sidney with a sweet post to Instagram. “This past year has been the most magical year of my life for one reason. That reason is you baby Sidney. To watch you grow, learn and become a little man is the greatest joy I have ever felt,” she wrote in the caption.

She continued: “The most magical bond I have ever experienced is the bond I feel with you. Before you, love was just a word that I thought I understood. NOW I truly understand what love is,” she continued. “I am forever indebted to you for teaching me the beauty meaning and truth of love. What more can I say other than I love you.”
