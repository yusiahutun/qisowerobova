Sabrina Carpenter's most stylish red carpet moments

 Posted on June 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  378 words  |  Martina Birk

American singer Sabrina Carpenter has the internet wrapped around her little finger. News that she would be embarking on her first-ever European tour had fans desperately trying to secure their tickets and now rumours of her romance with Shawn Mendes has sent people into meltdown. While her relationship status remains unconfirmed, what we do know for sure is that Sabrina is something of a red carpet luminary. The Disney star-turned-chart-topper is all about bold cut-outs, heaps of embellishment and a dash of sheer and we are totally here for it.

Hello! Fashion shares Sabrina Carpenter's most stylish red carpet moments:

The Nonsense singer put in a seriously stylish appearance at Vanity Fair's 2023 Oscars after-party. Sabrina opted for a striking Paco Rabanne gown featuring a sheer nude top, complete with bejewelled pasties, and a figure-skimming white ruched skirt.

The former Disney star hopped on the London Fashion Week-adored 3D floral trend last month at Universal Music Group's Grammy after-party in a knitted bra and matching thigh-split skirt, both adorned with rhinestone roses.

Sabrina was giving Barbie beach babe vibes at Spotify's Best New Artist event, donning a zig-zag printed crop top and a sheer pink sequin mini skirt over striped briefs. The finishing touch? Baby pink platform wedges. Margot Robbie would be proud.

The singer changed tack at the 2022 Fashion Awards, forsaking her usual beloved sheers, cut-outs and daring lengths in favour of a muted orange Alberta Ferretti hooded number.

Eliciting some major outfit and blow dry envy in one fell swoop, Sabrina looked stylish at the 2022 American Music Awards in a silver fringed ensemble by Oscar de la Renta and metallic platforms.

The On My Way singer was Barbiecore personified at the 2022 Venice Film Festival in a spangly, slightly puff-shaped pink mini dress and neon single-strap platforms.

Sabrina was a retro dream at the 2022 MTV VMAs in a floral bead-encrusted halter gown featuring daring flank cut-outs. It's giving "Groovy, baby!" – and we're not mad about it.
