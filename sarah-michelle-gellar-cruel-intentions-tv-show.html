Sarah Michelle Gellar To Star in Cruel Intentions Tv Series

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  203 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Last October, NBC announced it would be reviving the '90s classic Cruel Intentions in the form of a new TV series reboot. Today, more good news was announced for fans of the cult-favorite film—Sarah Michelle Gellar will be back and reprising her role as twisted mean girl Kathryn Merteuil. The actress shared the news on Instagram in a photo with the film's producers, Roger Kumble and Neal H. Moritz, who are also back on board for the NBC revival.

"That's right "everybody loves me, and I intend to keep it that way" #kathrynmerteuil is back!!! I am happy to say that I will be joining them (and @nbctv ) on the new #Crueltv," Gellar captioned the photo. 

Does this mean we can expect cameos from Reese Witherspoon and Selma Blair, too? This is going to be good.
