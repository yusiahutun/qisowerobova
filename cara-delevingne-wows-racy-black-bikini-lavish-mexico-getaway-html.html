Cara Delevingne showcased her incredible figure in a racy black swimsuit as she posed for sizzling snaps on Instagram while enjoying a lavish getaway in Mexico. 

The model, 30, wowed in the strappy cut out one piece as she displayed her peachy behind while relaxing in an infinity pool overlooking the ocean on Thursday. 

She pulled her long blonde tresses up in a high ponytail while enjoying the sunset at the 5 Star Luxury Resort & Spa in Los Cabos. 

Cara took to her Instagram Story as she donned a chic red patterned shorts co-ord and hid behind a chunky pair of sunglasses as she made her way around the resort. 

The actress opted for a pair of sliders and left her long blonde tresses loose as they cascaded down from under a dark bucket hat.

Cara is counting down the days until her docuseries, Planet Sex, premieres in the US on February 14 after airing on the BBC in the UK late last year.

The six-part series follows the supermodel as she explores her own 'journey to self-discovery' while traveling the world to discuss the topic with other people. 

The actress and model identifies as 'openly queer' and has been romantically linked with Harry Styles, Miley Cyrus and Leonardo DiCaprio. 

In recent years she has been linked mostly with women, including singer St. Vincent, actress Ashley Benson and singer Leah Mason, known as 'Minke'.

In a trailer for the show Cara said: 'I date men but, phwoar, do I love women and everyone else. In my own life I definitely had a lot of internalised homophobia and shame.'

Later, at a Pride parade, she exclaims: 'I'm just living off this queer energy'.

Also releasing in February is the second and final season of the steampunk-themed Amazon Prime series titled, Carnival Row, which Cara stars in.
