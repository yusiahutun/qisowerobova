The 9 Best Jo Malone Perfume Scents Ranked and Reviewed 2024

Best Herbaceous Scent

Jo Malone London Basil & Neroli Cologne

Best Herbaceous Scent

Jo Malone London Basil & Neroli Cologne

Credit: Nordstrom

This writer counts the brand's Basil and Neroli Cologne as one of the best scents of all-time. Its earthy fragrance combines basil with neroli, white musk, and orange flower for a fresh scent that also feels warm and elegant.

Customer review:

"I love most Jo Malone fragrances but this is hands down my fave. I wear it mainly spring/summer time as it is more of an almost beach-y scent. Two sprays and it lasts most the day on me." —Goldie33285968

TopBasil grand vert
HeartNeroli
BaseWhite musk

Best Woody Scent

Jo Malone London Wood Sage & Sea Salt Cologne

Best Woody Scent

Jo Malone London Wood Sage & Sea Salt Cologne

Credit: Nordstrom

Wood Sage and Sea Salt is ideal for the person who loves smelling as clean as possible. It even features notes of sea salt and sage for that coveted, fresh air smell that's always in season.

Customer review:

"This [perfume] smells like heaven on earth. If you love woody, earthy but still subtle scents, this is for you. I absolutely love it and have gotten so many complements on the perfume in the past few days at work and with friends. 100 percent recommend." —farihabeaute

TopAmbrette seeds
HeartSea salt
BaseSage

Best Fruity Scent

Jo Malone London English Pear & Freesia Cologne

Best Fruity Scent

Jo Malone London English Pear & Freesia Cologne

Credit: Nordstrom

If you're looking for a fruit-forward scent that's perfect all year round, consider Jo Malone's English Pear and Freesia Cologne. The top-rated fragrance contains a hint of woody notes that helps it stand out from a pack of traditionally fruity scents.

Customer review:

"Much has been said about the effect of fragrances; Jo Malone's cologne of English pear & Freesia transports me from the ritual of everyday to a peaceful place of gentle joys, a deep feeling of well-being and light. It lingers on the entire day - not obtrusively - but sends little reminders of beauty in simplicity." —Barbara68

TopKing William pear
HeartFreesia
BasePatchouli

Best Floral Scent

Jo Malone London Peony & Blush Suede Cologne

Best Floral Scent

Jo Malone London Peony & Blush Suede Cologne

Credit: Jo Malone

Jo Malone's Peony and Blush Suede fragrance is a hit among beauty lovers who gravitate toward floral fragrances, since it plays well with other scents and isn't overly sweet.

Customer review:

"This is the first floral perfume I have ever smelt and fell in love with. I'm not usually a fan of these types of smells, but this one is so light, airy, and fresh that I couldn't say no to keeping it. It isn't too overpowering and leaves a gentle fragrance to your skin." —Emma181299

HeartPeony

Best Fresh Scent

Jo Malone London Orange Blossom Cologne

Best Fresh Scent

Jo Malone London Orange Blossom Cologne

Credit: Bluemercury

Here's an orange blossom-powered option from Jo Malone that's as zesty as it is fresh. If you're not quite all in on fruity fragrances, this one's inclusion of musky orris and balsamic vetiver help keep it balanced while adding depth.

Customer review:

"This is a light, floral and orange scent that never overpowers but stays on throughout the day. I love this perfume!" —Kathy

TopOrange blossom
HeartWater lily

Best Light Floral Scent

Jo Malone London Wild Bluebell Cologne

Best Light Floral Scent

Jo Malone London Wild Bluebell Cologne

Credit: Bluemercury

Inspired by nature and particularly wildflowers dressed in morning dew, this beloved fragrance smells like a fresh-picked bouquet housed in a 100 milliliter-size perfume bottle.

Customer review:

"This was a beautiful, delicately floral, slightly sweet, light and airy, everyday wearable fragrance. It smells like all of my cottagecore dreams—basking in the sun in a white dress in an open field of wild flowers. This is one of my new favorite scents." —Nicknamelol

Best Barely-There Scent
