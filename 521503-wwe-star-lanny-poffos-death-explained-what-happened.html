Who was Lanny Poffo?

Lanny Poffo, also known as "Leaping", Lanny Poffo and "The Genius", was a Canadian-American professional wrestler, motivational speaker, poet, and actor. His successful career, which began in 1974, saw him compete in different levels of professional wrestling.

Some of the promotions he represented include the NWA, ICW, MSW, CWA, WWF, WCW and WWE. The wrestler retired from professional wrestling in 2020.

Lanny Mark Poffo was born on 28 December 1954 in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, to Angelo Poffo, a wrestler, and Judith (Judy) nee Sverdlin. He died on 2 February 2023 in New York City, USA, aged 68. The star had one sibling, a brother known as Randy "Macho Man" Savage, who died in 2011.
