Brooke Burns bio

Brooke Burns was born on March 16, 1978, in Dallas, Texas, to Brad Burns and Betsy Burns. She has American nationality but is of mixed race (English and Scottish).

Brooke developed an interest in ballet dancing from a young age. She started dancing at three years old and was active for 12 years. Unfortunately, her dream of becoming a ballerina was terminated when she got injured in a skiing accident at 14 years old. The accident resulted in a tear on her anterior cruciate ligament.
