Zuku Kenya internet packages and connection prices

 Posted on January 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  373 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Zuku is one of the best companies in Kenya that provides both Internet connection and digital TV. Zuku uses fiber technology to offer fast, reliable, and unlimited internet connectivity to its users. Currently, Zuku Fiber is available in most parts of Nairobi and Mombasa.

Zuku fiber comes in two different internet packages namely; business/commercial packages and residential/home packages. Below are examples of the residential Zuku Internet packages and their Zuku internet connection prices:

Home basic package

The user will enjoy internet speeds of 30 Mbps, and the package costs Kshs 4,699.

  • Home preferred package: This package costs Kshs 5,999 with 50 Mbps Internet Speeds.
  • Home premium package: With unlimited internet speeds of 100 Mbps, the package costs Ksh 9,999.
  • Home advanced package: This package offers 250 Mbps Internet Speeds at the cost of Kshs 19,999.

Apart from these, there are internet packages offered mostly in Thika Road and Embakasi area. The cheapest internet-only plan goes for Kshs 1,299 Kenya shillings and gives you a speed of 2 Mbps. Another internet-only plan offers speeds of 5 Mbps at Kshs 2,199. Lastly, internet users can also opt for the 10 Mbps internet package that costs Kshs 3,499.

It is important to note that internet users who opt for the 4Mbps package get a free set-top box but the subscriber is expected to pay a Zuku internet connection fee of Kshs 2000.

All the other packages come with a free set-top box, a free telephone, and a free Wi-Fi modem. The 30Mbps, 50Mbps, and 100Mbps each attract a Zuku fiber connection fee of Khs 5,999. Also, apart from these packages, Zuku offers businesses various options to enjoy extra services but at a cost.
