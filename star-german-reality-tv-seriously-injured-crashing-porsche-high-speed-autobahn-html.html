Star of German reality TV show seriously injured after crashing Porsche at high speed on autobahn

 Posted on June 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  597 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

A German reality TV star has been seriously injured after her Porsche was involved in a high-speed crash on the autobahn yesterday.

Julia Jasmin Ruehle, an influencer who was a star on the TV series 'Berlin: Day and Night', was driving her Porshce 718 Cayman on the autobahn towards Prenzlau when she lost control and hit the central barrier. 

The crash was so violent that the car rolled several times, littering the road with debris and losing three tyres in the process before coming to a stop.

Ruehle, 33, was able to free herself from the vehicle but suffered extensive injuries.

The crash took place last night at roughly 9pm local time on the A11 autobahn in the district of Barnim.

Police told the media that the car had impacted the barrier at such a speed and angle that it rolled several times and was totally destroyed in the process.

Upon arriving at the scene, police and ambulance services discovered the reality star's autograph cards scattered across the road.
