Robert Hardister's crimes

Robert Hardister from Florida is infamous as a habitual offender. According to The NZ Herald, he has been arrested at least five times in Palm Beach County alone, as well as in St Johns and Martin counties.

In 2012, he was arrested and jailed for nine months for motor vehicle theft. On February 15 2013, he was arrested again by the Tequesta police officer for an out-of-town warrant. Two days later, the authorities arrested him on numerous charges, which included burglary.

In July 2013, Robert was arrested for violating his probation. Roughly two months later, he was arrested on September 23 of the same year when the police officers found him sleeping in an allegedly stolen pickup truck in a Walmart parking lot.

After the arrest, he claimed to have borrowed the truck from one of his friends and paid them $20 to move some furniture into his new house. He added that he wasn't aware that the pickup truck was stolen. In June 2017, he was arrested for stealing a Chrysler and violating his probation.
