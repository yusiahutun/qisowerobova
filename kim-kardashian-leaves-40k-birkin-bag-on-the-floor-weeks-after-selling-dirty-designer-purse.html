Kim Kardashian leaves $40K Birkin bag on the floor weeks after selling dirty designer purse

 Posted on January 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  361 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kim Kardashian has come under fire for leaving a $100K Birkin bag on the floor at a basketball game. It comes a month after she was slammed for selling a ‘dirty’ Hermes accessory on Kardashian Kloset.

Fans are going wild after Kim Kardashian left her designer handbag courtside at a sports game. She is already believed to have crossed a line in February for selling a “dirty” Hermès purse for $65K.

Kim Kardashian’s Birkin bag drama

Km secured a front row seat at the Los Angeles Lakers game with her son, Saint West, but it seems she couldn’t quite get a seat for her Hermes Birkin bag – which ended up being placed by her feet.

On Saturday, the SKIMS founder was seen courtside at the basketball game, watching the NBA team take on the Golden State Warriors. By her feet was the pricy, investment bag costing a whopping $40K.

A similar design is currently being sold by Sotheby’s for $55K. Kim paired the accessory with a plain white t-shirt and black leather trousers, with heels, and appeared to place her bag on her knee at one point.

When The Kardashians fans spotted Kim’s bag on the dirty floor, they were shocked. The floor was littered with sticky popcorn for a start, and as a result, fans are not pleased with her recklessness.

“That bag cost more than most people pay in yearly rent,” one person commented on a photo that showed Kardashian at the basketball game. “Disgusting.”

Another commenter simply wrote, “Birkin on the floor is insane,” as a fellow social media user replied, “I wouldn’t put my LV on the floor let alone a Birkin but that’s just me.”

She sold ‘dirty’ Hermes purse for $65K

Kim sold a Hermes purse for just under $66K on Kardashian Kloset in February, which fans said was dirty. It was one of many owned by the Hulu star, who has reached a billionaire status of wealth.

The rare grey matte alligator was listed as “good condition” but a description of the handbag said there was “some discoloration on handles and underside bottom corners, and minor scratching on metal.”
