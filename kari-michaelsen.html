Biography

Kari Michaelsen was born in New York City, NY on November 3, 1961. During the 1980s, Michaelsen played Katie Kanisky on the NBC sitcom Gimme a Break! Her other television credits include guest star roles on Hart to Hart, Full House, and The Incredible Hulk. She co-starred with Nell Carter on Gimme a Break! During her childhood years, she lent her singing voice to the Sesame Street soundtrack and performed in several Off-Broadway productions.
