Some people might call it a "problem" when they see my bathroom, but I'm a heat-seeking missile for clean skincare. It used to just be skincare, period, until I learned about all the unnecessary and hormone-disrupting ingredients that frequent products I once loved, and resolved to clean up my beauty act for good. Now, coming across clean skincare formulas like Pacifica's Dreamy Youth Day and Night Face Cream makes my heart light up — and according to the shopper reviews, it's an anti-aging powerhouse.

While there are no official standards for "clean" beauty products, I interpret it as cruelty-free products formulated without hormone-disruptors like parabens and phthalates, skin sensitizers like harsh essential oils, MI, MCI, and formaldehyde, and ingredients derived from crude oil, like petrolatum and mineral oil. The end game: products that are both good for us, and not-destructive for the Earth (love you, Earth. Cue the Donna Lewis).

Pacifica's anti-aging moisturizer hits all the right notes, thanks to the formula's combination of plant oils, peptides, shea butter, hyaluronic acid, plant extracts, and vitamin E. Most importantly, it gives results: 50-year-olds write that it's the best moisturizer they've ever used, making their skin more luminous and reducing the fine lines around their eyes.
