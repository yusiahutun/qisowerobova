Ennik Somi Douma (born March 9, 2001), better known by her Korean name Jeon Somi (Korean: 전소미), is a Canadian-Dutch-Korean singer-songwriter based in South Korea. Jeon shot to domestic stardom as the first-place winner of the competition series Produce 101 and a member of the series' project girl group, I.O.I. Following the conclusion of I.O.I's group activities, Jeon ventured out as a solo artist. Under The Black Label, Jeon debuted as a soloist on June 13, 2019 with the single "Birthday", and released her debut studio album XOXO on October 29, 2021.
