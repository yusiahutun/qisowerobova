Jon Rothstein is an American journalist and sports host famously known as “College basketball’s Hungriest Insider”. He is well known and respected for his work with the show College Hoops Today and also for his years of contribution to well-known sports and media networks.

Early Life

Jon was born on 11th September 1983 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA but has not shared any information or detail about his parents. However, it is known that both his parents were journalists. He is believed to be a Christian of white Caucasian ethnicity and is also believed to be a single child. Jon’s early education and school life have not been disclosed but he is believed to have completed his high school education with great results and is also known to have been an ace in sports. Later, for his college education, he attended Ithaca College where he actively played college basketball and was one of the most recognized alums of his college.

Jon Rothstein Career

Jon started his career as a journalist and host when he first joined ESPN radio in New York. He started working with the channel as a radio host as well as a writer for the network’s online site and meanwhile, he also joined the MSG network. Jon works as a host of College Hoops Today with Jon Rothstein on the CBS sports network and has been working with the network since 2010. He has been an active and significant part of the network’s basketball coverage and is extensively dedicated to his job. Other than his, he releases a daily column on college basketball titled The Breakfast Buffet on the College Hoops today platform where he shares his insight and daily updates year-round.
