Are Tom, Katie Still together From Winter House? Where They're Now

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  523 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

As the second Tom and Katie to have a romance on Bravo, fans have been desperate for information on if Tom Schwartz and Katie Flood are still together from Winter House Season 3 and where their relationship is now that filming has ended.

Tom and Katie are two cast members on Season 3 of Winter House, Bravo’s reality TV series following a group of Bravo stars from various shows as they vacation in the snow. Tom starred on Seasons 1 to 10 of Vanderpump Rules, while Katie starred on Season 6 of Below Deck Mediterranean. Winter House, which is a spin-off of Bravo’s Summer House set in The Hamptons, New York, premiered in 2021 and has since featured more than a dozen Bravo stars from across shows like Southern Charm and Below Deck.

The Winter House cast for Season 3 includes eight main cast members from six Bravo shows: Vanderpump Rules, Summer House, Below Deck Mediterranean, Below Deck Sailing Yacht, Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard, and Family Karma. According to Entertainment Tonight, Vanderpump Rules star Tom Sandoval was set to be a part of the Winter House cast for Season 3, however, he was cut after news broke of his affair—now known as Scandoval—involving his ex-girlfriend, Ariana Madix, and their co-star, Raquel Leviss. The site also reported that Tom Schwartz, Sandoval’s Vanderpump Rules co-star, also almost missed Winter House filming due to Scandoval, which required cameras to pick back up for Vanderpump Rules Season 10.

“There is a possibility the duo could go to Colorado later, and come in near the end of the Winter House season. Producers are now reaching out to some of the previous Winter House cast to see if they can join, and asking part-time people to now be full-time,” a source told Entertainment Tonight in March 2023.

But back to Tom and Katie. So… are Tom and Katie still together from Winter House Season 3? Read on for where Tom and Katie from Winter House are now after cameras went down in Colorado.

  • Are Tom and Katie still together from Winter House?

    Are Tom and Katie still together from Winter House 2023? The answer seems to be no. Tom Schwartz (Vanderpump Rules) and Katie Flood (Below Deck Mediterranean) are seemingly not still together after Winter House Season 3.

    Tom confirmed he’s not still together with Katie after Winter House Season 3 and is single in an interview on the “When Reality Hits with Jax and Brittany” podcast in June 2023, which was recorded after Winter House Season 3 finished filming. Tom also confirmed that he was not dating Jo Wenberg, his friend whom he was rumored to be dating on Vanderpump Rules Season 10. “I’m not dating anyone. I don’t have a secret girlfriend that lives with me. I’m single and not ready to mingle,” he said. “I think [Jo]’s hilarious, beautiful, talented, Jo’s the sh-t. I just don’t want a girlfriend. I just got out of a 12-year relationship.”

    Tom also confirmed that he’s still “not in love with” with his ex-wife, Katie Maloney, whom he divorced in October 2022, though he still “loves her” and they’re “cordial.”
