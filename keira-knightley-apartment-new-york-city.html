Inside Keira Knightley's $6 Million NYC Apartment

 Posted on June 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  198 words  |  Martina Birk

Keira Knightley's New York City home has just sold for $6 million, though we're unsure why the actress would want to leave the place—it's downright stunning. The property is placed in Manhattan's trendy TriBeCa neighborhood, boasting two floors, three bedrooms, two and a half bathrooms and 3,820 square feet.

The place has an overall modern air, with geometric wooden accents, but a delicate floral wallpaper in the master bedroom and rustic wood features make it feel romantic and homey, too. Plus, there's plenty of space inside and outside of the apartment, with 23 feet ceilings and a 900-square-foot private patio.

The property, which Knightly moved into in 2015, was ultimately sold under Keller Williams NYC. Take a look at the rooms below.
