Nick Cannon's $200k budget for taking kids to Disneyland is enough to buy an entire house

 Posted on October 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  287 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Nick Cannon is father to 12 children, so there’s no doubt he spends a lot on them, and he’s recently revealed how much he spends each year taking them to Disneyland. It’s a lot.

Although the TV host has definitely got his handful, after welcoming his 12th in February, the star said having more kids could still be on the cards. “God decides when we’re done,” he stated.

Nick Cannon reveals how much he spends on taking children to Disneyland

Making an appearance on The Breakfast Show, the 43-year-old revealed he attends the adventure park once a month, taking his children for birthdays and holidays.

Of course, with 12 children, this adds up to a lot of dollar, and he’s revealed exactly how much.

“Do you know how much money I spend at Disneyland a year? To move around Disney, like, I’m probably spending $200,000 a year at Disneyland,” he said.

“Disneyland is expensive already off [the] top if [you’re] trying to stay in the hotel. It’s not how it used to be. You gotta make reservations,” the star continued.

However, we’re sure this isn’t as big of a problem for Mr. Cannon as it sounds, with an estimated net worth of $50 million, Celebrity Networth reports.

The star shares an adorable Christmas card

Getting into the festive spirit, The Masked Singer host, and Miss Universe Guam, Britney Bell shared several photos from their family’s holiday photoshoot on Instagram.

The pair share three children, Rise Messiah, Powerful Queen, and Golden Sagon. 

The family can be seen stunning in sparkly gold outfits, posing in front of a huge Christmas tree, embellished with gold decor.

Captioning the post, Cannon exclaimed: “The Holidays are here!!”
