Kendall Jenner Went Braless in a White Cropped Tank and Spring-Worthy Green Trousers

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  307 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

If Kendall Jenner's latest pair of pants don't put you in the mood for spring, not much else will. The model stepped out in Los Angeles on Thursday, in a breezy but chic outfit that ushered in the change of season.

Kendall exited her SUV wearing a white tank top that she cropped by folding the bottom portion over the top, sans a bra. On the bottom, she chose vibrant green, baggy trousers that were held in place with a thin brown leather belt to match her early 2000s-nostalgic platform thong sandals. She carried a black leather bag over her shoulder and accessorized with thin, rectangular sunglasses. Her newly-dyed red hair was worn straight and parted down the middle.

Kendall also shared the look on Instagram with a gallery of images and videos. In one snap, she sat on the ground, while other videos showed off her grade A modeling skills.

Earlier this week, the reality star hit the town with her boyfriend NBA star Devin Booker and their fellow couple friends Hailey and Justin Bieber for a cute double date. For dinner at Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, Kendall opted for a black mesh spaghetti tank top and flared leather pants.

Booker recently made a subtle nod to his model girlfriend in an interview with the Wall Street Journal. When asked if having the spotlight on his personal life was hard he replied, "I wouldn't say hard. Honestly, I'm enjoying life to the fullest, and that came off my tongue so easily because it wasn't always this way, but I feel like I'm in a good place right now. I love my family, I love the people around me, and I love the impact that I get to have on this world, the younger generation under me and the kids who look up to me."
