Tracee Ellis Ross causes a stir in high-waisted pants and glittering bodysuit

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Tracee Ellis Ross is no stranger to a daring fashion moment, and earlier this week the actress proved her sartorial prowess in a suave all-black ensemble.

The Black-ish star, whose mother is the legendary Diana Ross, took to Instagram to delight her 11 million followers with a series of sultry selfies posing on the stairs of her stunning LA home. Tracee, 50, looked incredible as she rocked a pair of seriously high-waisted black pants that hugged her feminine silhouette. See more of her daring style moments in the clip below...

The star added a black bodysuit adorned with a glittering, embellished belt, and layered with a tuxedo-style cropped jacket. 

It was all about the accessories for the actress, who levelled up her midnight-hued attire with statement gold jewellery, including a skeleton-like necklace and dramatic chandelier earrings. 

As for makeup, the star highlighted her beautiful features with a flick of feline eyeliner, peachy blush and soft pink lip.

"I like to take pictures of myself when I get home," she admitted in the caption of her Instagram post.

Tracee then detailed each snap, admitting to fans that she does indeed "use the face app (but only a lil bit)." 

The Golden Globe winner added: "Can anyone spot which item I stole from the closet of Joan Carol Clayton many moons ago?" to which her fans unanimously guessed it was the golden purse she clutched in the last photo. 

Tracee's fans were totally floored by her beauty, flooding the comments of her post with compliments. "Because when you look this good…. DO IT!" wrote one fan, as another sweetly shared: "I’m taking style notes!" 

"We love a homemade Tracee photoshoot," gushed a third fan.
