Chlo Sevigny Lets Her Lacy Skirt Peek Out From Under Her Peacoat

 Posted on April 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  321 words  |  Martina Birk

There should have been a runway dedicated to Chloë Sevigny’s looks from the past week, because she’s been pulling out all the stops, on both the red carpet and the streets of New York City.

Thursday morning, the Big Love star was seen leaving Good Morning America in an all-black ensemble that put a fresh spin on winter dressing. Sevigny made her winter peacoat the core of her outfit, letting it act almost like a coatdress, were it not for a tiny bit of her lace skirt peeking out from underneath.

MEGA//Getty Images

The black outerwear featured a wide lapels and two rows of tortoiseshell buttons down the center, as well as two buttons off to either side of her chest. Sevigny paired the piece with coal-colored slingback heels and a lizard-embossed leather handbag from Vivienne Westwood, with a golden version of the brand’s signature orb logo attached to the front. To round out the fit, she sported rimless sunglasses with jet-black lenses.

Gotham//Getty Images

Sevigny also wore the coat a day prior, that time styling it with a patchwork denim skirt, lamé Gucci socks, and brown brogues. You could also see a sky-blue button-down under her jacket.

Besides her clear adoration for this coat, the actor has been showing her love of bows this week. While promoting her upcoming FX series Feud: Capote vs. the Swans, the Oscar nominee wore a cream Christopher John Rogers dress with the material tied into a giant bow on the front, as well as a black strapless Simone Rocha minidress, the entire top of which was formed by an oversize bow of fabric.
