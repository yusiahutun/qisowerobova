Key Takeaways Gaming IEMs offer advantages such as portability, noise isolation, and clear voice communication for multiplayer, making them ideal for mobile gaming and on-the-go scenarios. However, compared to gaming headphones, IEMs lack spacious soundstage and their audio signature tends to be more neutral, resulting in a flatter sound. Choosing between IEMs and gaming headphones depends on priorities: immersive soundstage and tailored gaming audio favor headphones, while mobility, voice chat clarity, and noise isolation favor IEMs. [Read More]
