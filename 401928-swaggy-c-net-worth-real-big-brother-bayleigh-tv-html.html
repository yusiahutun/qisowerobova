Who is Swaggy C?

His childhood was not rosy as they encountered many financial challenges that rendered them homeless twice. His dad was battling kidney failure by then and couldn’t afford full-time employment due to his deteriorating health.

Christopher managed to attend Kolbe Cathedral High School and later enrolled at Saint Peter’s University. While growing up, Williams had a keen interest in basketball and was a successful high school court player.
