I used to steer clear of body scrubs because the ones I had tried were far too rough and abrasive. However, once I found a gentle formula, it was game over. If you’re hesitant when it comes to this product category, it might be time to try Nativa SPA’s Madagascar Vanilla Soothing Body Scrub.

The key ingredient to highlight in this body scrub is Madagascar vanilla. It’s more than a tasty baking ingredient; it’s chock-full of antioxidants that soothe skin. Plus, Nativa SPA sources its Madagascar vanilla sustainably from Madagascar Island in Africa. Mix it with the brand’s 100 percent pure quinoa oil and you’ve got a cocktail of ingredients that your skin will want to drink up immediately. If you’ve never heard of quinoa in skincare, just know that it’s rich in protein, fiber, and healthy fats that help moisturize, firm, and smooth skin.

After you use this body scrub, your skin should have a natural shine and feel smooth and comfortable rather than red and scratched up. Using this product also helps your next steps—oil and lotion—absorb into the skin better, making increased hydration another benefit of rubbing this goop all over your body.

I can’t forget to mention the Madagascar Vanilla Soothing Body Scrub carries the most heavenly scent of warm, sweet vanilla and citrusy, refreshing lemon. Not only will your skin feel brand-new, but you’ll be smelling like a million bucks wherever you go.

This body scrub works just like its counterparts. Apply it all over your body, rubbing in gentle, circular motions. Let your skin soak up all of the ingredients and then rinse off. Nativa SPA suggests doing this weekly for 28 days to see the best results.
