Best Beyonce Cowboy Carter Lyrics: Daughter, American Requiem

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1427 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It’s the day we’ve all been waiting for since she stepped out at the Grammys wearing a crisp white Stetson. And while some purist crybabies draw issue with the fact that it’s being called a country album, the best Beyoncé’s Cowboy Carter lyrics and musical arrangements are the very definition of country music. So sue us.

This epic 27-track album, a follow-up to her beloved Rennaisance, was released on March 29, 2024. The teaser on her website included her driving through a desert road and the 27-track album is an homage to her home state of Texas, not without that signature Bey spice, though.

Country music often revolves around themes of love, heartbreak, rural life, family, and everyday experiences, which “LEVII’S JEANS” and “II MOST WANTED” have covered. Traditional country music typically features instruments, like acoustic and electric guitars, banjos, fiddles, harmonicas, and sometimes pianos or organs. Ahem, “TEXAS HOLD ‘EM”, “ALLIGATOR TEARS”, “FLAMENCO”. And country lyrics are often narrative-driven, telling stories or conveying emotions, which you can observe in “DAUGHTER”, “16 CARRIAGES” and “II HANDS II HEAVEN”. Without further ado, here are the best Beyoncé’s Cowboy Carter lyrics. (All lyrics courtesy of Genius.com)
