Gospel music

When Green released The Belle Album in 1977, his record sales decreased. As a result, he desired to shift to a gospel minister. He was then ordained to preach. His music passion continued, and between 1981 and 1989, he recorded several gospel albums and other productions, including:

  • Lord Will Make a Way (1980)
  • Your Arms Too Short to Box with God (1982)
  • He Is the Light (1985)
  • Soul Survivor (1987)

Green established the Full Gospel Tabernacle Church in Memphis in 1976. Today, he resides and ministers in Memphis, close to Graceland. In addition, he belongs to an African-American wing of Freemasonry known as Prince Hall Masons at the Thirty-Third Degree.
