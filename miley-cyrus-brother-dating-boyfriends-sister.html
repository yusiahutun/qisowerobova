Miley Cyrus' brother Braison is dating Patrick Schwarzenegger's sister Christina

 Posted on January 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Aldo Pusey

    Miley Cyrus has been loved up with her boyfriend Patrick Schwarzenegger for the last three months, and it appears the 22-year-old has now played matchmaker for her younger brother Braison, as he is rumoured to be dating Patrick's sister Christina. According to the new couple have been seeing each other since December. Miley confirmed that she was dating Patrick, the son of Arnold Schwarzenegger, in November after weeks of speculation. Now it is believed that Braison began dating Patrick's 23-year-old sister a month later. Budding actor Braison has shared two pictures of himself with Christina on his Instagram page over the last two weeks. In one snap the new couple can be seen cuddling up to each other in a picture that he captioned with a heart emoji.

    Braison Cyrus shared a picture of himself and Christina Schwarzenegger on Instagram

    While details of Braison and Christina's relationship are unknown, it is likely they were introduced through their famous siblings.News of the blossoming romance comes as Miley and Patrick's relationship appears to go from strength to strength. The couple have been inseparable over the last few months and are reported to have spent Christmas and New Year together with Patrick's father Arnold Schwarzenegger and his girlfriend Heather Milligan. A source told : "Arnold approves of his son's new relationship, and he was the one who suggested they all spend New Year's Eve together in Las Vegas."

    Miley Cyrus and Patrick Schwarzenegger have been together since November

    Despite reports to suggest otherwise, Patrick's mother Maria Shriver is also said to approve of the couple's relationship. "It's untrue that Maria is unhappy," a source told. "If Patrick's happy, she's happy."
