Sky Sports appear to play f*** Chelsea chants during Fulham vs Brentford

 Posted on March 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  504 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Attentive viewers got more than they bargained for during Saturday’s game between Fulham and Brentford as several noticed Sky Sports had failed to muffle out an x-rated Cottagers chant.

In a tense west London derby, fans were treated to something of a cagey affair in first half at Craven Cottage.

But it appears they were a little distracted by the superimposed chanting on Sky Sports broadcast, with some fans noticing one anti-Chelsea song in particular.

Sharing their revelation on Twitter, several viewers claimed they heard a chant of “We are Fulham, we are Fulham, f*** Chelsea”, which Sky had clearly initially forgotten to muffle out or remove before playing it live on air.

Journalist Steve Lillis wrote: “Are my ears shot? Sounds like @SkyFootball are pumping out “we are Fulham, ???? Chelsea,” as part of the background noise.”

“They left ‘F*** Chelsea' in the chants so it seems ok to me,” a fan added.

Another put: “Twice I've heard so far is the "we are Fulham, we are Fulham, f*** Chelsea ".....comical!”

“Anyone else loving the fact that sky are pumping in the “we are Fulham, f*** Chelsea” chant #ffc,” a third added.

While a fifth pointed out: “Hearing f*** Chelsea over the tannoy.”

“Fantastic crowd noise even playing ‘f*** Chelsea’ clearly not run through compliance #FulhamFC,” a jubilant sixth wrote.

Some of the chanting was later muffled by the broadcasting giants.

Commentators were also forced to apologise for swearing on the pitch at one stage, as tensions ran high in southwest London.

Fulham boss Scott Parker said before the game that he was pleased there would be fake crowd noise both inside Craven Cottage and on TV.

"Yeah I liked it, we used it and tested it at the Cottage," Parker told the Evening Standard.

"I liked it, it brought a bit more of an atmosphere into the stadium and I think it could be a slight advantage, even if it is just a little bit of noise in the background.

"It is something we are looking at and will make a call on in the next day or so and see where we go."
