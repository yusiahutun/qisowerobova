Man suffers life-threatening injuries Saturday night when gunfire rings out

 Posted on June 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  170 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Update: A gunfire incident in which a 26-year-old man suffered life-threatening injuries shortly after 7:30 p.m. Saturday remains under investigation.

Davenport Police responded to the parking lot of Castlewood Apartments, 2130 Emerald Drive, for a report of gunfire, Davenport Police say in a news release.

Additional 9-1-1 calls indicated one person had been shot. Upon arrival, officers located a scene in the parking lot and a man suffering from a life-threatening gunshot wound.

The injured man was transported by Medic EMS to a hospital. Two vehicles and two apartments also were struck by gunfire, the release says. No other injuries were reported.

The incident remains under investigation.

Earlier: A heavy police presence remained on the 2100 block of Emerald Drive, Davenport, in the Castlewood Apartments complex after a gunfire incident about 7:45 p.m. Saturday.

Police recovered at least nine shell casings and a gun at the scene, where a car had crashed and sat with its doors open. Officers placed crime-scene tape in the area and spoke with neighbors.
