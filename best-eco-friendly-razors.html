How should I use one of the best eco-friendly razors?

Safety razors include a sharp blade (or blades) attached to a reusable handle. Because they can offer such a close shave, you should proceed with caution. To help prevent nicks and cuts, follow the below steps:

  • Prior to shaving, apply a thin layer of oil or shaving cream to your skin. This will ensure that your skin stays smooth and irritation-free.
  • Don’t apply pressure. The head of these safety razors are weighted, so you don’t need to press into your skin while shaving. Many manufacturers also recommend holding the safety razor at a 30-degree angle.
  • Use shorter strokes. Take your time, especially at the beginning, and avoid dragging the blade too much. In between strokes, rinse your razor blade to get the best shave possible.
