George Michael's California home is up for sale for 5million

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  761 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The stunning California mansion owned by the late George Michael is up for sale at £5million - just days after his former lover Fadi Fawaz was arrested for trashing the Wham! singer's London property in Hampstead.

The luxurious home is currently on the market for USD $5.995 million (approximately £4.936 million), House Beautiful reported.

Located in the foothills of Santa Barbara, where many of Hollywood big names live, it sits on 5.7 acres and features five spacious bedrooms and six bathrooms across two floors - a total of 4,840 square feet.

The property was designed by Cliff Hickman who drew inspiration from designer Frank Lloyd Wright's Prairie Style with low pitched roofs and overhanging eaves combined with modern hexagonal shape.

On the upper floor, the living space's central chimney acts as a point of attraction and entertainment in the space.

Glass doors open up to a wrap-around terrace that offers a breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and the Pacific ocean.
