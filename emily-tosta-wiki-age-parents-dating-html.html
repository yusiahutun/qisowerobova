Wiki(Age)- Nationality

A native of Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, Emily was born as Emilia Gabriela Attias Tosta in 1998. The runway model blows the birthday candle on the 26th of March. At the age of 12, Emilia moved to Miami, Florida with her family and later settled in Los Angeles, California.

Her nationality is Dominican and possesses a mixed ethnicity (Lebanese, Venezuelan, Brazilian, and Italian.) The 21 years old TV actress weighs around 53 kg (117 lbs). As for her height, Emily is modest with 1.57 m (5 feet 2 inch) tall.

She got homeschooled and completed her high school education graduating in 2016.
