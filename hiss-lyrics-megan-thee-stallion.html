Nicki Minaj Diss, What She Said

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  965 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The girls are fighting! With her newly anticipated single out, “Hiss” by Megan The Stallion’s lyrics is a total jab to all her haters out there. Some of those lyrics might particularly be directed towards some of the most famous rappers in the scene right now.

Megan teased the track in a video days before it’s release“I just wanna kick this s–t off by saying, ‘F–k y’all!” she exclaims in the clip. “I ain’t gotta clear my name on a motherf–kin’ thang. Every time I get mentioned, one of y’all b—h a– n—as get 24 hours of attention. I’m finna get this s–t off my chest and lay it to rest, let’s go!”

“Hiss” lines up with the snake theme of Megan’s last solo release, 2023’s “Cobra.” That track served as her first independent single since leaving and settling a long legal battle with 1501 Certified Entertainment.

So what does “Hiss” by Megan Thee Stallion lyrics mean?

“Hiss” by Megan Thee Stallion meaning

“Hiss” by Megan Thee Stallion is a straight diss track to all her haters out there. She even says it bluntly in the first couple of lines. However, she is also very specific to the people who use her for clout.

“I feel like Mariah Carey (Mariah Carey), got these n—s so obsessed (Damn) / My pussy so famous, might get managed by Kris Jenner next (Mwah),” she raps ferociously in the first verse, referring to Kardashian momager Kris Jenner.

One line that got the public’s attention is the line, “These hoes don’t be mad at Megan, these hoes mad at Megan’s Law.” The line refers to Megan’s law which makes it necessary for law enforcement to share information about registered sex offenders with the public. It could be interpreted that the line was directed towards Nicki Minaj‘s husband, Kenneth Petty, who is a convicted sex offender.

Things have been on the rocks for Megan and Nicki as the “WAP” rapper is known to collaborate frequently with Cardi B, who isn’t on good terms with the Pink Friday 2 aritst.

Nicki Minaj’s Response to “Hiss” by Megan Thee Stallion

After the song was released, Nicki Minaj immediately went on an Instagram Live where she reacted to the line by directing a diss towards Megan.

“Bad b—h she like six foot, I call her Big Foot/ The bitch fell off, I said get up on your good foot,” she rapped.

Nicki also said during the broadcast: “You have three Grammys and you have to learn how to rap on the beat and be comfortable in the music.” As to Megan’s reaction, she posted a photo of herself laughing on her Instagram story.

Nicki’s fans, known as Barbz, went online to react to the diss and support Nicki. “Big Foot in the clubs would go crazy,” one fan posted and the tweet was reposted by Nicki. One fan even asked Nicki to post a photo of her foot and she posted her manicured foot.

However, Megan’s fans, known as Hotties, also defended the “Body” rapper. “megan thee stallion has literally stayed silent as literally everyone has spoken on her but when she finally responds defending herself it’s a problem?,” a fan posted.
