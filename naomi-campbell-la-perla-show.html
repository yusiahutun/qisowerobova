Naomi Campbell Walked the Runway at La Perla Fashion Show

 Posted on March 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  250 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Getty Images

On the first night of New York Fashion Week, La Perla brought its A game with a strong model lineup led by none other than Naomi Campbell. The supermodel opened the runway show in a lacy blue slip dress, floor-length robe and furry heels (which is the only outfit I want to wear from now on).

Campbell wasn't the only major model to walk the show though. Isabeli Fontana, Joan Smalls, Sasha Pivovarova and La Perla brand ambassador Kendall Jenner also hit the runway (Jenner closed the show leading the finale walk).

Despite the fact that the iconic '90s supermodel has never actually retired from the catwalk, her appearances on the runways these days are pretty rare. As with every other time that Campbell walks, the super managed to steal the show with not only her general presence, but also her famous walk:
