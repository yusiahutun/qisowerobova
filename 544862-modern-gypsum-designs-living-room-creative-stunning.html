Gypsum designs for living room in Kenya

Are you craving a fresh look for your living room? Gypsum offers a beautiful and practical way to elevate your space. This readily available material allows for stylish and creative designs that complement your living room aesthetics. Here is a list of modern gypsum designs for living rooms.

1. Coffered ceiling

Coffered ceilings are sunken panels that can be assembled in various ways to create shapes like rectangles, circles, or squares. They're among the simple gypsum designs for living rooms. As long as your ceiling isn't low, this design can make it appear taller. It opens space, and the crisscrossing beams separate the room into segments or add visual length.

2. Gypsum ceiling with LED lights

Gypsum LED panel lights are lighting fixtures that have replaced conventional fluorescent ceiling lights. They are among the best lighting options for living rooms. While LED panel technology has been widely used lately, it is relatively new to the market and is gaining ground because of its innovative features and sleek look and design.

3. Multicolour gypsum ceiling

The defining feature of this ceiling design is the use of different colours. Each panel or segment of the ceiling can be painted in distinct hues depending on the living room interior design theme or personal preference. Bold primary colours can create a playful, dynamic effect, while subtle pastels create a more sophisticated, serene atmosphere.

4. Circular design

The circular gypsum ceiling design will add an artistic element to your living room. This design typically features a central circular panel made of gypsum board, which can be either recessed or protruding, depending on the desired effect.

Additional decorative elements, including intricate plasterwork, stencilling, or integrated lighting, can enhance the circular ceiling design.

5. Flower design

The flower design will transform your living room into a visually engaging space by incorporating floral motifs and patterns into the ceiling architecture. It is achieved through detailed craftsmanship, where gypsum is cut into shapes that resemble flowers.

The floral designs are often accentuated with strategic lighting that casts soft shadows and highlights the intricate details.

6. Symmetrical ceiling

The symmetrical gypsum ceiling design features geometric shapes like squares, circles, or hexagons. These elements are arranged to reflect each other across a central axis, creating a visually satisfying uniformity.

This design approach is popular in both classic and contemporary living room interiors. Integrated lighting will highlight the symmetry while illuminating the room uniformly.

7. Gypsum ceiling with chandelier

Gypsum, known for its smooth finish and versatility, is often moulded into intricate patterns or clean geometric shapes to add aesthetics. In this design, the chandelier hangs gracefully, serving as both a focal point and a source of ambient lighting.

In addition, the gypsum's reflective properties help to amplify the chandelier's light, creating a bright and inviting living room atmosphere.

8. Gypsum ceiling with fan

Are you looking for gypsum designs for living room with fan? Well, there are lots of them. This design is practical for maintaining comfort while integrating seamlessly into the overall design theme. You can choose a fan that matches or contrasts with the gypsum's colour and pattern, making it as much a decorative element as a functional one.

How much does a gypsum ceiling cost in Kenya?

The cost of gypsum ceilings in Kenya ranges between Ksh. 2,000 to Ksh. 3,000 per square metre based on each particular design from simple to complex.

Wrapping up

There are several modern gypsum designs for living rooms, and they are among the expensive ceiling solutions due to the bulkheads and crown moulding involved in crafting them. Besides the multiple materials used to craft gypsum ceilings, technicians usually charge higher installation prices because the designs are time-consuming and messy.
