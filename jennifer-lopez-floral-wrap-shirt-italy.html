Jennifer Lopez Pairs Floral Wrap Shirt with Lacy Short Shorts While in Italy

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  270 words  |  Martina Birk

Jennifer Lopez's honeymoon style streak isn't over yet.

The star was recently spotted in Capri, Italy continuing her European summer excursion. During one outing, she wore a silk kimono from Camilla featuring gilded buttons, an abstract umbrella pattern, and cheetah print on the sleeves. Satin pink short shorts with a black lace trim matched the top.

Other accessories included pink circular sunglasses, gold TKEES flip flops, and a black Valentino rock-stud bag. She kept the look effortless with a messy high bun and natural glam.

MEGA

The star and her newlywed husband Ben Affleck had recently been honeymooning in Paris, where they brought along their blended family: J. Lo's 14-year-old twins Max and Emme, whom she shares with ex-husband Marc Anthony, and Affleck's daughters, 16-year-old Violet and 13-year-old Seraphina, whom he shares with ex-wife Jennifer Garner. Affleck has reportedly since flew back to the States this week.

Throughout the holiday, Lopez has stepped out in a number of summery European looks, from Reformation sundresses to feminine floral statement pieces.

More recently, the "Let's Get Loud" singer looked radiant in a pair of flare jeans and a navy Dior sweater with floral detailing. She accessorized the ensemble with aviator sunglasses, and a sage green, alligator printed Hermès Birkin. Meanwhile, Affleck coordinated in a gray T-shirt, navy pants, and Golden Goose sneakers.

The two stars were married just over a week ago, tying the knot in a small surprise Las Vegas ceremony attended by their kids.
