Maren Morris Debuts Short and Sassy New Haircut

 Posted on September 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  280 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In addition to her music, Maren Morris is also known for her bold style, and she is officially ready to take on spring after chopping off her long locks.

Sharing the process and after photo via Instagram, Morris shows off her recent haircut that gives the singer a fresh look, complete with a short, bob-like hairdo. Framing her face nicely, the new cut features side swept bangs that reach just under her chin.

"New woman :)" the "My Church" singer writes as a caption, with the photo flaunting her locks.

Fans are clearly in agreement, with many posting positive comments. "Absolutely love it," one Instagram user writes, while others dish out compliments calling Morris' look "gorgeous," "beautiful" and "pretty," with another commenting that it's an "empowered hair cut."

Morris' boyfriend — fellow country singer Ryan Hurd — likes it, too, writing on his own Instagram page: "Daaaaaaaaaaaaaamn."

But the new look isn't the only fresh change Morris has going for her. She's featured on Thomas Rhett's new single, "Craving You," that's set for release on Friday (March 31), hot off the release of the deluxe edition of her No. 1 album, Hero, which features three new songs.

The "I Could Use a Love Song" hitmaker is also an early winner at the 2017 ACM Awards,  taking home the trophy for New Vocalist of the Year. She'll have to wait and see if she scoops up awards in the other categories she's nominated in, like Female Vocalist of the Year, Album of the Year and Single Record of the Year when the show airs on Sunday (April 2). She'll also take the stage to perform at the 52nd annual awards show.
